NYU, Stern - 2008 Application Deadline Change

Our first deadline has moved up from December 1st to November 15th. The second and third deadlines remain the same.  Below are the deadlines and initial notification dates.

1st Deadline
Application Deadline: November 15
Admission Notification: February 15

2nd Deadline
Application Deadline: January 15
Admission Notification: April 1

3rd Deadline
Application Deadline: March 15
Admission Notification: June 1

The Fall 2008 full-time application and the new first deadline are posted on our website.  As always, we encourage international applicants to apply by the first deadline for priority consideration for scholarships and off-site interviews.
