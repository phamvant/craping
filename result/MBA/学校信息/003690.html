Duke MBA Info Session Summary（12.6 BJ）

昨天去参加了Duke MBA Info Session， 下面是宣讲的总结。hope this helps 没参加的同志们。
没有记录个人提问部分，仅仅是AO宣讲和alumni tips。 若有错误遗漏希望有人补充呀，我不想误导别人啊。

Application tips from AO

WE：

  • Show now is the time 、MBA is a logical next step
  • Lead project（outside work leadership OK）

Resume

  • 1 page only
  • Show career promotion and impact
  • Look professional
  • Internship experience OK but not essential

Academics

  • GPA
  • GMAT（to normal questions like whether to retake the exam,the answer is if you think you can do better，just take it）
  • GRE accepted

Recommendation

  • Choose wisely（someone can give us highlights about you AND have time to do so）
  • Don’t copy。。。（I miss this part, did he mention that if you write it for them and they copy into the system, AO will definitely know that？）
  • Explain if you cannot choose recommender in current setting（where to？optional essay OK？I missed this too somebody pls help）

Essay

  • 25 random：
    • lshow you are fun people
    • la chance to know about you
    • loutside work ok
    • lsee others’s list on the blog
  • why duke
    • lShow your homework about duke
    • lShow why duke is best fit
    • lShow your passion and energy
    • lBetter to mention TEAM FUQUA
  • optional
    • Explain about recommender choice, low GPA, gap， never show something like why you chose current industry or career

Interview

  • A chance to know you
  • Dress professionally
  • Ask smart questions
  • Follow the question asked
  • Do not practice answers
  • Be yourself
  • Do homework
  • Show fit
  • Relaxed

Tips about life in duke from alumni

The hardest thing to do as Chinese & regrets & soft skills

  • Be humbly aggressive, present yourself, speak up, gain confidence,
  • Know about social context such as sports（football and baseball）
  • Step our your comfort zone and network
  • Smartly use your advantages and contribute to the team
  • Network with someone with similar context first（jp, south Korean）
  • Push yourself to improve language skills
  • Try something new, always challenges, out of comfort zone
  • Know yourself, what you want, where to develop your skills , think it through , listen to yourself and pick one and then network from day one
  • Get to know people and stay focused
  • Know your strength and weakness, do not compete others s strength with your weakness; you will be fine
  • Work hard, play hard
  • Do not worry about your face, just speak up
  • Try to find something you are good at, be well prepared and contribute, then you will be popular as team member
  • Network with non-Chinese
