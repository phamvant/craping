FUNDAMENTALS OF MANAGEMENT
管理学基础
A Yale Pre-MBA Program
以耶鲁大学管理学院的经验，专门为潜在商学院学生定制的咨询项目
August 3-14, 2015
2015年8月3日至8月14日

TEASER
概要

Renowned Yale School of Management professors will expose you to management fundamentals and show you how MBA training can help you to achieve your career goals, make an impact on society, and become a transformational leader in your chosen professional sector.
耶鲁大学管理学院的知名教授们将为参与者揭示管理学的基础知识，及商学院教育是如何帮助参与者达成职业目标，对社会产生积极影响，并在选择的职业领域成为变革型的领导者。

This two-week program for recent graduates (with less than three years of professional experience) and current university students who aspire to become global leaders for business and society will help you understand global business and hone your leadership skills, while exposing you to the rigor of MBA training.
这个为期两周的项目主要为两类人群设立：有梦想成为全球商业领导者的,少于三年工作经验的本科毕业生,以及有此志向的在读本科生。这个项目将会帮助参与者了解严格的商学院教学模式，同时理解全球商业环境，并历炼领导力。

OVERVIEW
项目概况

FUNDAMENTALS OF MANAGEMENT, Yale’s pre-MBA program in Beijing, will help you achieve your goals in business and develop your leadership skills in ways that benefit you, your profession, and society. The insights you will obtain will familiarize you with the value of an MBA education that emphasizes rigorous analysis and decision-making in a complex, interconnected global marketplace.
耶鲁大学在北京设立这个管理学基础—专门为潜在商学院学生定制的咨询项目，是为了帮助参与者达成职业目标，发展符合自身条件的，利于职业规划的，及对社会有积极影响的领导能力。通过此项目，参与者会更深刻的理解，强调培养分析及决策能力的商学院教学模式，在一个复杂的并且相互关联的全球市场中能起到的重要作用。

Business and Management
商业和管理

You will engage with key business concepts drawn from Yale’s MBA program in areas such as accounting and finance, entrepreneurship, global economics, marketing and strategy.
参与者将有机会接触耶鲁大学管理学院MBA项目的一些核心商业概念，比如，财务及金融课程，自主创业课程，全球经济课程，市场营销及战略课程中的核心概念。

Leadership and Impact
领导力及影响力

The Yale School of Management’s mission is to educate leaders for business and society. We help you build your leadership skills and capacity, so that you can develop a successful career that has a positive impact on your chosen sector.
耶鲁大学管理学院的办学宗旨是为社会及商业领域培养领袖。我们将帮助项目参与者建立领导技巧及能力，以确保参与者在选择的职业领域内有良好的职业发展。

Company Visits
公司访问

Participants will visit leading companies in Beijing to explore how concepts from the MBA program are implemented in real life.
参与者将有机会在北京对一些龙头企业进行访问，探究商学院课程的核心概念是如何在现实环境中实践的。

Getting ready for the MBA
为MBA做准备

Hear from experienced Yale experts how to best position yourself for a selective top MBA program.
参与者将有机会聆听来自耶鲁大学的资深专家讲解如何为进入顶级的商学院深造做准备。

Networking
人际网络

FUNDAMENTALS OF MANAGEMENT will provide you with networking opportunities with other participants, businesses in Beijing, and the greater Yale community.
管理学基础项目将为参与者提供与其他参与者，北京的企业，及广泛的耶鲁团体进行连接互动的机会。
