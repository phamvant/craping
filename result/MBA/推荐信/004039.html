1. How long should the recommendations be?  Which style is better, detailed or concise?

Within 1000 words  is prefered. The specific style of the letter depends on the recommender who will write the letter. Either style is acceptable.

2. Should it provide supporting examples for all recommended virtues?  How many expamples are enough for each point?

Examples make the points sound. If an example can the point clear, it\'s enough; otherwise, three still make no sense.

3. Should the two recommenders address different aspects of you? Or should they both emphasize certain strength to make it look more coherent?

It depends on what the recommenders think of you. Either is okay.
