I received error messages whenever I uploaded documents but if you click on "continue" in the window with the error message, you\'ll be brought back to the application screen.

Check if you can see the "view" and "delete" buttons under the "upload document" button. If you can see your document when you click on "view", you should be safe. Alternatively, you can click on the "print application" button on the left navigation bar to see your application in pdf format. I could see all my uploaded documents in the pdf file even though I received error messages when uploading.
