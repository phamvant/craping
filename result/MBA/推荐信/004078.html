Just dont concern severely with this kind of cases. A western professional will never recommend a guy she/he doesn\'t appreciate. Actually "straightforward" recommendation words will not ruin your reputation but strengthen it, since adcoms in b-schools enjoy most reading a REAL person with personal characteristics and diversities, with both convincing advantages and actual disadvantages. While Chinese recommendators, or even applicants themselves, are good at or only capable of composing flattering letters, which will only be thrown into rubbish bins after reviewing - as adcoms will have been enduring hundreds of similar fake letters each year from China.

So just have thorough discussions with your supervisor, share with her your thoughts on personal characteristics, how to strengthen your advantages and fade your disadvantages, and be fully confident against those overstated counterparts from Chinese. :)
