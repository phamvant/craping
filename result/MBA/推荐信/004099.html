Don\'t go with titles. Choose people who can write about your accomplishments and growth in great details.

It would be great if you can find someone with good title and knows you well, but most of the time, the rule of thumb is go with someone that can write strong and convincing recommendations and gives specific examples of your development instead of someone with a big title only.
