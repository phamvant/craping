刚拿到JohnsHopkins的Offer + 整理的JHU MBA面试topics

还记得是5月9日面的，刚好5月中旬到美国出差，之前还纠结于是否争取5月下旬到巴尔的摩Campus Interview，结果由于录取周期的原因，被校方婉拒了。

面试的是Julian，可以在LinkedIn看到他的相关background(推荐大家了解面试官的一个好方法)，之前根据校友Bill（ID：justbp）的建议，搜罗了能找到JHU的面试题目，结果面试时全部命中。本觉得把握很大，但由于网络状态的原因，最终影响了面试的质量，5月11日得到了学校通知，我被列到了waitlist里面去，说最终的答复要等到6月1日才有。在此给大家一个小提示，如果信号不好，一定要果断把Skype关掉，调好信号再继续。

Waitlist对于我来是是一个最纠结的选项，我搜索了CD上面关于如何逃离waitlist的方法，除了技术层面的问题，最大的要领就是坚持、不放弃、展现出你的诚意。其间Bill给了我很大的鼓舞和帮助，我到美国出差期间还和Bill打了一个电话，他给了我很多建议，还专门为我写了一个推荐理由的陈述mail给了Julian，起到了非常大的帮助。同时，我也把相应在工作中能够证明我professional working experience的材料（Training Certification, Awarding, Job description & International conference certification）附上，专门写了一封邮件给Admin office再做了进一步的争取。

在此也把我面试过程中理出的Topic分享给大家，并继承Bill学长的精神，回答大家感兴趣的问题，祝所有CDer心想事成！

  • Tell me more about yourself,Current work duties
  • Is there anything you want to stress in your resume?
  • Why mba?
  • From where you hear Carey?
  • Why Global MBA program, why Carey, What parts of Global MBA program attract you most?
  • short-term, long-term goals
  • What kind of company do you want to work at? name one
  • three characteristics your friends will describe you
  • Things you did recently
  • Any hobby, anything you do in your spare time
  • What are your strengths and weaknesses
  • Do you have any plan to improve the shortcomings you mentioned in our program?
  • What do you think of good leader? What kind of leader you want to be?
  • any team work experience in your work
  • What are characteristics of a good teammate?
  • How do you manage a team, what do you think is most important to manage a team?
  • Describe a situation that you in a team collaborate with team members and persuaded them to do XX?
  • what do you think of a successful team
  • if someone in your group disagrees with you, what would you do
  • Many of our candidates have overseas work experience, do you think you can adapt to the study if you are admitted?
  • Any international experience, like traveling outside china?
  • What business, in your opinion is humanistic business?
  • Most important career accomplishment?
  • Is there anything you want to add to your application?
  • Any questions?
