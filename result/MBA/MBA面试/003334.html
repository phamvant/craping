面经

  1. Walk through resume 2-3 minutes.
  2. Leadership case.
  3. Why mba? how mba helps you? why emory?
  4. Contribution to program.
  5. Leadership style.
  6. How colleagues think of you?
  7. Career goal? primary, secondary.
  8. How to integrate into the community?
  9. Choose the company when graduated from university? or have a plan?
  10. Why switch the industry?
  11. Any questions.
