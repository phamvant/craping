USC Marshall MBA 面经 [2021.12.09]

趁着刚刚面试完还新鲜来跟大家分享一下，面试时长是 30 分钟，面试官是 laura.面试是 blind interview.

  1. Walk me through your professional career after college.
  2. Career goal.
  3. Why MBA?
  4. Why Marshall?
  5. Your strength? Something that you are naturally good at?
  6. What about your weakness?
  7. What do you do for fun?
  8. Q&A.
