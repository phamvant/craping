USC Marshall MBA 2020 Fall R2 面经 With Mona [2020.02.22]

今天终于面试了 USC, 是 onsite interview. 面试我的是 Mona, 是一位 MBA year 2 的在读学生。 大概面试了 45 分钟。

  1. Self-introduction
  2. Tell me about your work
  3. Why MBA
  4. Why now
  5. Short term and long term goal
  6. Why Marshall
  7. Challenges at work
  8. Teamwork experiences that are not at work
  9. Tell me a time you make an unpopular decision
  10. What 3 words your friend describes you
  11. What will you contribute in Marshall
  12. Misconception about you
  13. Q&A

Mona 人很好，面试的时候她首先示范了一次 self-introduction，所以轮到我的时候就很容易进入面试状态。整体氛围也是很 casual.

Timeline:

  • 01.05 application submitted
  • 02.10 interview invitation
  • 02.22 interview

最后祝各位心想事成。
