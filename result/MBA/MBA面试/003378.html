Rotman MBA 面经 With Agenes [2020.06.03]

女票申请的多大 mba 项目， 材料递交后一周通知面试 面试官 Agenes. 面试问题不难，准备了常规面试问题，大部分有被提问：

  1. Walk me through your resume.
  2. Why MBA/Rotman?
  3. Short/long term career goal.
  4. Describe occasion you have complete a complex task.
  5. Describe failure in life.
  6. Describe a time when you had conflict interests with your cookers.
  7. Describe a measurable achievement in your career.
  8. Any questions.

等待结果中。人品贴，希望尽快有好消息。Good luck!
