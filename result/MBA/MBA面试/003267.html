Notre Dame/ND/圣母大学 2020 Fall MBA/MSBA Dual Degree R2 面经 with Cassie [2020.01.30]

感谢 CD 对我申请的帮助。ND MBA/MSBA dual degree的面经比较少，希望这篇能帮到大家。

今天刚面完MBA/MSBA dual degree with Cassie. Cassie非常友善，也有经常follow up.  面试了40分钟左右。

  1. Why MBA/MSBA dual degree?
  2. Why now?
  3. Why Notre Dame, what you can bring to ND community?
  4. A leader (someone you met or haven’t met before) you admire?
  5. Post-MBA goal?
  6. Tell me more about your company?
  7. What do you do in spare time?
  8. Tell me more about the community service (因为上一个问题有说到spare time 有去做volunteer)?
  9. Three words to describe yourself?
  10. Any goal other than work?
  11. CASE STUDY: shoe company's revenue increased by 6%, market share decreased by 10%, why and what should do? (6-7分钟准备，对方摄像头关闭)
  12. Fun fact?
  13. Anything that is not in the application you want to share?
  14. Q&A?

最后Cassie有说R2是3月13日前有通知。

祝愿大家都拿到心仪的offer！

Time Line：

  • 1/7 R2 DDL 压线交application
  • 1/8 interview invitation
  • 1/30 interview
