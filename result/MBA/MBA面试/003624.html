Schulich 面经 [2023.11.07]

Kira: 不太记得口语问题了，something about diverse， 写作是向一个教授发邮件了解 career fair 的信息 etc

真人面试：

  1. Tell me about yourself and your work in the recent 2 years.
  2. Why MBA, Why do you want to come to Schulich?Why Canada?
  3. The worst decision you've made. If do it again, what would you do?
  4. Any difficulties that you think you will encounter if you enter the program?
  5. Post MBA plan/Career Goal.
  6. What do you think you can bring to the school and the classmates?
  7. What you will do to reach out to someone you dont know yet.
  8. Something I wouldn’t know from your resume and application materials
  9. Leadership experience.

真人面试是 Elaine,一个特别 nice 的华裔小姐姐，之前是在 UT 念书，然后加入了 Schulich 负责本地招生，但是她讲因为今年国际生特别多，所以她也负责了部分国际生面试，anyways, 整个面试下来非常 conversational，最后聊了很多多伦多本地生活的事情，本来会议设置是 45 分钟，最后聊了 1 个半小时，哈哈哈，算是很不错的体验（比较 weird 的就是两个中国人一直讲英文哈哈哈），在聊 Why Schulich 的时候我也提到了 CD 论坛，因为 Elaine 之前也在北大 MBA 招生工作过，所以她对 CD 非常熟悉（不知道会不会看到我这个post lol）.
