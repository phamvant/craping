1.问的问题都很常规：

  • Walk thru resume.
  • Why Booth?
  • What courses and what professors?
  • Tell me a time when you persuade people do sth?
  • Tell me a time you learned something new and how did you apply?
  • Tell me a time when you collaborate with others?
  • Tell me a time when you had a challenging teamwork?
  • Tell me a time when something did not work as your plan and what you did?

2.下午刚刚面完和大家分享一下，大致的问题和之前大耗分享的非常相似

  • Tell me about yourself.
  • Why Booth?
  • Courses and professors.
  • What student clubs are you interested in?
  • Why MBA and how do you think MBA can help you for your long term and short term career goals?

3.我感觉四个没啥问题了，achievement，leadership，teamwork，conflict.结果估计她紧张问了两遍 leadership.

4.顺序可能不是这样的。凑合着看。我的面试官不管我说啥，都要 follow up 一个具体的事例。

  • Tell me about yourself?
  • What kind of working environment do you like the most and why?
  • What role do you play in a team? Answered depends on the situation. Followed up with detail examples as both team leader and team member.
  • What is your biggest improvement area?
  • Tell me a situation when you have to ask for help?
  • Tell me a situation when others ask you for help?
  • Post MBA goal and how does Booth can help achieve the goal?
  • Why booth?
  • Share with me something you haven’t talk about.

5.题目：

  • Walk me through your resume.
  • Why did you choose your first job and division?
  • Describe a time when you experienced cultural shock at work?
  • Describe a time when you learnt from your colleagues and changed your course of actions?
  • Describe a latest setback at work.
  • Why MBA now given the opportunity cost?
  • Why Booth?
  • What do you value most when selecting MBA programs?
  • Post MBA goal? (give me some names of employers in your desired post-MBA industry)

6.问我的问题，除了上面提到过的还有 what do i do for fun?

7.题目：

  • Tell me about yourself.
  • Why mba?
  • LT and ST goals.
  • Why Booth?
  • A situation where you had to manage multiple priorities. (follow-up)
  • A situation where you had to persuade a teammate?
  • A situation where experienced a challenge and how did you tackle it?
  • How will you contribute to the booth community?
  • Anything else you would like the AO to know?

11.赶在年前完成了 Booth 的面试，校友是位毕业 12 年的精品投行合伙人大佬，人特别 nice，全程 45 分钟，面试 35 分钟，Q&A 10 分钟，面试问题如下，供后续小伙伴参考，并祝大家好运！

  • Quickly introduce yourself in 2 minutes.
  • What's your professional experience within a multinational environment, and what you have learned from it.
  • What you have grown the most in your past xx year experience in xx company.
  • What's your biggest regret and if you do it again, what will it be.
  • What are your short-term and long-term career goals.
  • What have you done to pursue your career goals.
  • Why do you apply to MBA at this point of time.
  • What researches have you done about Chicago Booth.
  • Why Booth.
  • What contributions can you bring to the Chicago Booth community.
  • If you can only choose one hobby, what would it be and why.
  • Q&A

13.刚面完 booth，给大家一些面经：

  • Tell me about yourself.

  • Questions about my personal achievement.

16.投行 pe 背景，二年级学生，好 structured 一面试官。

  • Self-introduction
  • Interview Questions

Part I: Word description

  • How do your colleguages/manager describe you? 3 words
  • How do your friends/family members describe you? 3 words
  • Something you want to stop doing, start doing, continue doing.
  • Area of improvement.
  • 忘记了应该至少还有一个题。

Part II： Behavioral question (expect a story)

  • Disagree with others.
  • Get help from others.
  • Surprising constructive feedback.
  • You bring you perspective to a situation.
  • Challenge.

Part III:

  • Career goal.
  • Why MBA?
  • Why Booth?

还有一道如果不读 mba 怎么计划的？
