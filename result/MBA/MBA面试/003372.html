Cambridge MBA 面经 [2020.05.08]

回馈 CD,希望后面有更好的 offer,分享一下剑桥 skype 和奖学金 kira 面经。

剑桥 skype 面试是和 faculty member,记得的问题是：

  1. Why Cambridge?
  2. What you are interested in the curriculum?
  3. How covid 19 will impact the world economy? What are the futures of US and China after covid 19 and the trade war?
  4. Innovative ideas?
  5. Failures encountered?
  6. Weakness?
  7. Questions for him?

Kira 面试：

  1. Contribution question
  2. Leadership experience
  3. Why are you interested in S&T?
