INSEAD 面经 [2021.08.29]

INSEAD 校友 1

  1. Career goal.
  2. Why MBA now?
  3. How do you solve disagreement in a team from your work experience?
  4. How do you manage high pressure?
  5. Can you share an example on your international experience.
  6. Can you share an example to organise an event?
  7. Why INSEAD What other school did you applied? What is your preference?
  8. How do you know about INSEAD, from which sources?
  9. Why do you choose Singapore as home campus?
  10. Can you share a cross-culture communication example at work?
  11. How does your colleagues describe you?
  12. Can you share a leadership experience?

校友 2

  1. Introduce yourself.
  2. Career goal.
  3. Why mba?
  4. Why INSEAD?
  5. Why 1-year program?
  6. What other school you applied? Why not US MBA school?
  7. How did your multi-cultural experience help you on cross-cultural communication?
  8. What is your 3 strength and 3 weakness?
