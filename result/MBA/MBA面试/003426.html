UCLA Anderson MBA Interview 面经 [2020.11.03]

An interview with a year 2 student. The interview was about 30 mins.

  1. Go through resume.
  2. Why MBA now?
  3. How will the Anderson network help you?
  4. How will you contribute to the Anderson network?
  5. What role are you planning to take in the student club (EA)?
  6. Short & Long career goal?
  7. Plan B?
  8. Why Anderson program?
  9. Tell me more about how Anderson's program will help you achieve your career goal?
  10. What classes are you planning to take?
  11. Working experience in a diverse team.
  12. How do you deal with a different opinion from your team member?

多写两句我的感受：感觉 Anderson 的同学非常的亲切，但是问题没有太大的深度，比较的浅，无脑跟踪。可能是学生身份的原因，在这方面经验也不是很多。在过程中，我忘记了大肆强调 UCLA 的地理优势，失策失策，希望后面的朋友一定多提两句。
