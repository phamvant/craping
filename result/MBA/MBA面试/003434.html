Cornell Johnson MBA Interview [2020.11.12]

I just finished my interview with an admissions officer. The interview lasted 30 mins. Surprisingly, he didn't ask me any behavior questions.

  1. Introduce yourself.
  2. Why MBA?
  3. Why now?
  4. Have you spoken to any Johnson alumni?
  5. Why Johnson?
  6. Career goals?
  7. Plan B?
  8. Are you planning to come back to the Bay Area after the program?
  9. What student clubs are you planning to participate in?
  10. What's the biggest impact you want to make when you come to the program.
  11. Are you planning to take any classes before you come to the program? (I didn't expect this question. I said no, and asked why.)
  12. What else do you want to share with the AdCom?

面试官刚到 Johnson 工作不满一年，比较青涩。全程互动很多，没什么压力，轻松聊天。Johnson 是一个很欢乐的集体，面试中，面试官也非常在意我与在读生和毕业生的沟通情况。今年从 11.18-12.18 都会陆陆续续出结果。大家加油！
