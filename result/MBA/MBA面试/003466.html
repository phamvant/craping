Chicago Booth R2 MBA 面经 [2021.02.09]

Booth 的 AO 效率超高，周末都在加班 match alumni interviewer，01.30 收到面试邀请并在系统中提交了面试表，01.31 就收到了面试官的信息，预约的今天早上，赶在年前完成。

校友是位毕业 12 年的精品投行合伙人大佬，人特别的 nice，全程 45 分钟，面试 35 分钟，Q&A 10 分钟，面试问题如下，供后续小伙伴参考，并祝大家好运！

  1. Quickly introduce yourself in 2 minutes.
  2. What's your professional experience within a multinational environment, and what you have learned from it?
  3. What you have grown the most in your past xx year experience in xx company?
  4. What's your biggest regret and if you do it again, what will it be?
  5. What kind of work environments do you like the most and what's your role in it.
  6. What are your short-term and long-term career goals?
  7. What have you done to pursue your career goals?
  8. Why do you apply to MBA at this point of time?
  9. What researches have you done about Chicago Booth?
  10. Why Booth?
  11. What contributions can you bring to the Chicago Booth community?
  12. If you can only choose one hobby, what would it be and why?
  13. Q&A.
