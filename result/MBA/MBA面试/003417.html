CMU Tepper MBA 面经 [2020.10.12]

I interviewed with an AdCom on 10.12

  1. Tell me your current job.
  2. Tell me a time that you completed a job without enough information or resources.
  3. Tell me a time that you solved a problem across departments in the company.
  4. Tell me a time that you helped your coworker.
  5. Tell me a time that you found a better/easier way to do your job.
  6. How did you work with a team member with different culture background.
  7. Why Tepper?
  8. Career goals?

I finished all the interview questions in 20 minutes. Then AdCom and I discussed how Tepper helps students amid COVID.
