fabiano：

Please be calm down. A good Why Duke essay is based on a good understanding of the school.
I think you should firstly develop a better knowledge of this great school, understanding its unique team fuqua culture, marketing strengths, its global strategy and commitment to diversity. To gain this good understanding, you may talk with current students, read the online chat records and browse the Fuqua web site.

After a good understanding of the school, just summarize your background and see what you can contribute to Fuqua and what Fuqua can give you to realize your career goals.
