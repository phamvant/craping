Rotman 面经 [2023.03.07]

把废话删掉啦，直接放面试问题吧……祝大家都拿到想要的 offer！

  1. Self-introduction in 2~3 minutes.
  2. Why MBA?
  3. Why Rotman，是否申请了其它学校？
  4. 未来 5 年内想要学习的东西。
  5. Career goal.
  6. Set a goal but couldn't achieve it, lessons learned.
  7. Proudest achievement at work.
  8. Get impatient/frustrated with a coworker.
  9. In reflection of all your past experience, what is the biggest lesson you have learned?
  10. Questions for the interviewer.
