Kellogg MBA Interview [2021.01.28]

Kellogg interview questions: (自己面试遇到的问题以及面试群里小伙伴的分享）

  1. Walk through your resume
  2. Why MBA?
  3. Why now?
  4. What's your long term and short term career goal and why kellogg?
  5. What's your role in a team and leadership experiences?
  6. What is a weakness in your resume?
  7. How would you contribute to the Kellogg community?
  8. Anything else you want to share with me?
