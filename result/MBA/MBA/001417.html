Vanderbilt MBA 面筋

1月7号提交的申请，1月9号ddl，1月11号收到面试邀请，我看网上说因为报名的人少，估计人人有面试机会。2天前通过SKYPE面的，AO Bailey是我的面试官。面试是blind interview，面试官只有我的简历。一共30分钟。面试问题如下：

  1. 简单的讲一下简历。当中有停顿,AO问了一个问题。
  2. Why MBA? What companies you want to work for?
  3. Why Vandy?
  4. How do your coworks describe you? using 3 adjectives.
  5. How do your friend describe you?
  6. What constructive critism you have received?
  7. What's your largest achievement in a project?
  8. Give you 1 minute to talk in front of your boss why your boss should hire you.
  9. 2 things you want me to communicate to the Admission Office.
  10. What other programs have you applied for?
  11. Q&A.

差不多就是这些问题。祝大家好运~
