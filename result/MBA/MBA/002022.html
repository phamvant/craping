McMaster Full-time MBA 面经 [2020.01.26]

提交完申请才发现这个专业要四十多万人民币，超出预算辽，所以就没好好准备，破罐子破摔了。最新的面经回馈给大家-2020/1/26

一共四个oral questions，一个写作。

ORAL QUESTIONS:

  1. Describe your specific career aspirations and why a DeGroote MBA is the next step in your career journey.
  2. Tell us about your proudest accomplishment. More specifically, describe a project or activity that you led, and how did your leadership result in a positive impact on the business, organization or broader community.
  3. Share a challenging situation working with a diverse group of individuals (i.e. age, gender, culture, and ethnicity). What was the challenge, and what did you do to address it?
  4. hare with us your most proud achievement as it relates to volunteer work or community engagement. What was your role, what did you do and what were the outcomes?

WRITTEN TASK:

  1. What motivates you to put forth your greatest effort and to complete a task?
