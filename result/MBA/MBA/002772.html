University of Miami MBA小面一份 [2011-12-13]

面试官Aurora, 45分钟，原定30分钟好像面试的时候没有怎么看我其他的profile，就看了essay，和resmue

  1. 介绍下流程
  2. go thorough CV
  3. why you transfer from scientific research to business marketing
  4. what is the main process of marketing promoting event you did before?
  5. will you go back to your pervious company?
  6. what is your core competency & how to excel yourself?
  7. whether your company is aware of your application of MBA
  8. Will your company sponsor your MBA?
  9. what is your contribution to our program
  10. what do you expectation of MBA?
  11. Any questions for me?

今天不在状态，开始有点结巴，后来放开了，就好了。他们家今年的申请总人数和去年持平，但是国际学生的申请数量在上升。奖学金好像是50%-75%，评价方面是GPA，WE，GMAT
决定时间：2月1号，说是一般在1月会有结果，但是保守的话是在2月1号。
