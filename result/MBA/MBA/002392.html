Kellogg MBA 面经 [2022.01.20]

Wish me and everyone good luck!

  1. Walk me thru your resume.
  2. Why MBA?
  3. Why Kellogg?
  4. What are the short-term and long-term goal?
  5. What is your leadership style and what is the leadership style you dislike?
  6. What can you contribute to the Kellogg community?
  7. What do you like most and dislike most for your current job?
  8. What is the weakest part of your candidacy?
  9. Is there anything you need to add?
  10. Questions for me?
