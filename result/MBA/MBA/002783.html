2011年12月17日Minnesota Carlson MBA面经

CST时间早上9点，也就是北京时间晚上11点面试的，面试官是jennifer，人非常nice。之前几分钟是通过skype视频，无奈网络非常不稳定，一直断线，试了5分钟以后，果断改电话面试。

1) Tell me about yourself
2) Share a situation that you are faced with urgent deadline
3) Share a situation that you receive critical opinions from others
4) Share a situation that you cannot get clear or detailed directions from your supervisor
5) Share a situation that you demonstrate your teamwork skills (都怪LZ在前面3个例子离把自己夸的太英明神武了~~~~(>_<)~~~~ ，jennifer said that you seem to work well independently, could your give me an example to demonstrate your teamwork spirit?)
6) Short-term and long-term goals. Why Carlson
7) Any other schools you have applied for
8) Do you have any questions to ask

面试官人很好，我之前很紧张skype不停断线的事情，换了电话面以后，jennifer还不停安慰我说不用担心，不会影响面试结果~~

热腾腾的面经发完，面试的时候有特别谈到work replacement的问题，感觉上是一家非常down-to-earth和nice的学校吧~~bless！！！
