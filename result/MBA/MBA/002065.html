WUSTL MBA 面经 With Chris Collier [2020.02.15]

校园面，全程比较 nice，偶尔会根据回答再追问一点，但总体是按照 list 一个一个来的，全程一直在记录填表格。

我的问题基本如下：

  1. Tell me about yourself.
  2. Why mba why Olin?
  3. Short term long term goals? any particular company.
  4. What characteristics do you want to get in mba?
  5. What do you think of St Louis?
  6. How to choose school?
  7. Experience on leading team or building relationship/ partenarship.
  8. The influence of a Global trend on your industry.
  9. Creactive way to solve a problem.
  10. Ethical problem or tough decision.
  11. Useful feedback from others.

最后根据他对自己跟 career center 的介绍我问了几个问题。

我的个人 tips 是保持冷静和亲和，全程记得微笑，对方说话要聆听和反馈。
