Chicago Booth MBA校友面经 [2012-02-11]

一直从CD上获益很多，希望自己的经验对后来人有帮助

上周6在HK面的，校友非常nice，a conversational interview, 有部分问题还是在后来我问问题的过程中和聊天中问的。整个面试大概1个小时。

1. Walk through resume （a few further questions on my career choices）
2. ST goal
3. Why Booth
4. What quality of you are you most proud of
5. Tell me an example of you that you turn things back on track when they deviate from normality
6. What would your subordinates describe you as a leader
7. Your contribution to Booth
8. What if you are dinged by Booth, or even all B-schools that you applied
9. If you could live your life another time, what changes would you make
10. Other B-schools applied

Best luck to the folks waiting for both interviews and final decisions, including myself.
