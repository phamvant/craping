UBC MBA Skype面试 with Michael Holaday [2018.02.18]

回馈CD。

UBC的面试官Michael Holaday，人不错，总共面了半小时。

问题如下：

  1. Self-introduction.
  2. Career goal.
  3. What makes you different from others?
  4. Why school?
  5. Why MBA?
  6. Choose one for Global Immersion Experience – German, Japan, Kenya, and why.
  7. What can you contribute to the school?
  8. What character do a leader need?
  9. What if one of your team members could not meet your expectation?
  10. The book you recently read.
  11. Any question?
