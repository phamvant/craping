贡献刚做完的Kellogg Video Essay题目 [2017.09.22]

虽然题目是随机的，但我知道还是有小伙伴像我一样搜今年的题目搜不到而小心慌。

1. What would your friends say about you to a person you have never met? 粗略看了大家往年的分享，这题没有。
2. What career path are you interested in? How do you get there? Why is this program good for you?
3. Tell me about a time that you were overwhelmed by the workload.  How did you feel? What was the outcome? 这题也没有。

给自己攒人品，也祝大家好运！
