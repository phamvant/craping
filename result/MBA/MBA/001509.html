Insead 2018 Jan Intake R1 Video Interview 面经[内含题库]

刚刚完成了Insead的Video Interview，来小谈一下感受。

我自认为对于面试很有自信，但是这个Video Interview确实和面试感觉很不一样。这一点可能也是因人而异。但是我的建议是，一定提前练习，要多练，要把摄像头当成你的梦中情人来练，最重要的就是感觉要练对。

我在网上有看过一个叫Vince的人录的几段视频，推荐大家能看一下，链接在此：https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_rBsqgrFfHc&list=PLNeJs5Tvx34jq8VokRlsH8hGQ-g4M9LB8

其实，1分钟的时间很快，说不了什么内容，每个问题2~3个点就足够了，而且每个点只需要一两句话概括就可以。除非题目要你讲个example，那么最好讲一个100字左右的故事。

你说话时候的表情和举止很重要，一定要时刻保持微笑，保持和你的梦中情人的eye contact，保持自信，不管你卡住了还是迟钝了，都要恬不知耻的微笑。

讲话的时候要有自己的节奏，每句之间最好有停顿，不要“恩”“额”“啊”，安静1秒钟的效果更好。不要求快，快是一种紧张和不自信的表现。

内容要有层次，开始先来个5秒自我介绍。结尾再来个5秒结束。中间可以分两三个点来讲。

练习模式是可以无限次的，而且每次题目都不一样，一定要好好利用。

当然最重要的是多练，多练，多练！

下面汇总一下我遇到的题目，和收集到的去年的题目，希望对大家有帮助。最后希望大家都能拿到dream school的offer！！！

正式题目：

  1. Describe the actions and behaviours of a manager or supervisor that you have responded effectively.
  2. If you are given unlimited resources, what services or product will you company provide?
  3. How do you fight against stereotpyes and include sensitivity?
  4. How Insead would help you to achieve your career goal?

练习题目：

  1. Who inspires you and why?
  2. Describe a situation where non-verbal cues have helped clarify a complicated situation.
  3. Name a figure from the business world you admire and explain why?
  4. Describe a stressful situation that you did not handle well at work. What did you learn?
  5. Describe a time when you felt under a lot of stress. What did you do to resolve (or handle) the situation?
  6. What is your international outlook?

去年的题目：

  1. Why do you want to apply for Insead?
  2. What are you expecting to learn during INSEAD MBA?
  3. How your colleagues would describe you leadership style? Give an example.
  4. How would you fight stereotypes in a work environment?
  5. You are starting a new project with team members coming from different cultures and educational background. How would you make them meet?
  6. What do you do when someone comes to you with a problem?
  7. What was the most interesting project you have worked on? Why was it interesting to you?
  8. How do you keep track of your vision? Or that of your company?
  9. What according to you is required to start a successful business and then to run it?
  10. Describe one situation where you faced ethical dilemma? How did you resolve ? What factors did you consider while resolving it?
  11. What is diversity according to you?
  12. What is your management style?
  13. How did you build your international experience?
  14. What is leadership according to you?
  15. What is entrepreneurship according to you?
  16. Describe a cultural shock that you have encountered?
  17. How do you manage teams?
  18. What is your vision?
