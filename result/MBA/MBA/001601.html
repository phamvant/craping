Berkeley Haas 校友面经 [2017.11.14]

在上海校友面试Berkeley，大约30分钟。问题除了一些比较personal的外主要是以下几个。

  • Difficult situation in the workplace。
  • Why not other finance schools？Why Berkeley？
  • Teamwork in an inefficient teamwork setting。
  • What you want to address/improve at Berkeley Haas？
  • Career goal related questions。

Very thankful to have the interview opportunity and now fingers crossed！

Best Luck to All！
