Yale SOM MBA on campus interview [2011-11-18]

1. walk me through the resume
2. Tell me about one of the recent projects at work
3. Why MBA
4. Why now
5. Why Yale SOM
6. A specific question regarding my career goal, something like describe the current situation of XXX industry in China

Very typical MBA questions, I was not asked about high school experience, why ug major, why ug school, why first job, etc, which have been asked a lot by SOM in the past.
