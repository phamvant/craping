Rotman MBA Skype interview [2020.01.31]

攒个rp，今天刚刚面了Rotman MBA，Skype with Agnes。我是1月13申请，然后24接到面试通知。

  1. Walk through your resume in 2 to 3 mins.
  2. Clarification on my dual degrees. (我有两个本科，所以解释了一下）
  3. Short term and long term goal.
  4. Biggest failure.
  5. Biggest measurable achievement on your resume.
  6. Describe a time when you didn't have the authroity but you had to make decisions.
  7. Describe a time when you had conflict interests with your coworkes .
  8. When you had to learn something you didn't know before?

Q&A 环节聊了很多Agnes的经历，人特别健谈，给的经验和建议也非常到点上。

整个面试大概50分钟，其实前面问题只大概讲了25分钟后面都是我的问题。
