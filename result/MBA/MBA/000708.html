CEIBS SKYPE interview [2014-02-27]

  1. What progress have u made in the last N years in X company?
  2. What are the 3 most important take-away's from your working experience?

  4. Why u wanna quit your job and pursue MBA degree in CEIBS?
  5. Why do u have this entrepreneurial idea?
  6. How do u keep ur team learning in the rapidly changing business enviornment?
