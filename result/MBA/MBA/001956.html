Ryerson MBA 面经 [2019.07.03]

看到论坛上 Ryerson 的面试信息很少，上来分享一下。申的是 Ted Rogers 商学院 2017 full time MBA，项目只有一年。这个项目貌似只有人机面试，就像录 video essay 一样，没有真人面试的。

时间：30 秒准备，60 秒回答，表示完全不够用，说不完。

题目：

  1. Tell us about one way in which you are active within your community, outside of school and work. Why is this important to you?
  2. Tell me about a time when you mentored or helped another person achieve their goals. What did you gain from this experience?
  3. Tell me something about yourself that gives you a unique perspective. How does this help your approach to problem solving?
  4. Would you rather be given direction when starting a project or given free rein? Why?
  5. Tell me about a time when you mentored or helped another person achieve their goals.
  6. What did you gain from this experience?
  7. How does the Chinese economy affect the global economy?
  8. Tell us about your strategy for keeping multiple tasks prioritized. Have you ever become overwhelmed by your workload? If so, what did you learn about yourself?
  9. Talk about Globalization's advantage and disadvantage.
  10. How will technological innovation shape the coming future?
  11. What kind of characteristics do you think it's important in your field of study?
  12. Give an example of Q3.
  13. If accepted, how are you going to prepare yourself for this new challenge?
