面经：

General 的问题跟之前两所大幅度重复，因为是最后一个面试的学院，直接裸面，整个过程还是比较流畅愉快的。这里我就把最关键的问题放上来：

MBA Co-op Admission Interview Activity B

Scenario: You are attending a consulting workshop at the Ron Joyce Centre (Burlington campus). During the workshop you realize you would want to work at one of the firms a speaker represents. However, you haven’t noticed this firm posting any co-op roles on the co-op job portal. Given, that you have very minimal knowledge of the firm you still decide to approach the speaker.

Task: Following the scenario above, describe in two minutes; the skills, background/experience and qualities you would demonstrate to her (your brand statement); including why you would be a good candidate for a consulting role. You will be asked to present your statement at the end of your interview.

Time allocated: 20 Minutes
