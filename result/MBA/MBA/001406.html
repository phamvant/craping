Kollogg Video Essay 问题合集 （我三个都中了）

刚才做了 video essay，下面是我搜集的Kollogg video essay 的合集 （是整理了chase dream 里之前帖子-谢谢chasedream，以及各位前辈）

我这边考的三道题都中了 1. what animal you want to raise 2. Goal + Why Kellogg 3. example of stepping out of comfort zone.

我觉得如果有时间，好好准备一下下面的题目还是挺有帮助的。如果有新题欢迎回复。

  1. One question will be a “get to know you" icebreaker type of question.
  2. What is your most valued family tradition?
  3. Tell us a food that you'd like to have unlimited access to and why?
  4. Tell us a leader you admire and why?
  5. Most interesting class, why?
  6. If you were to go on an all-expenses-paid vacation next week where would you go and why?
  7. What is the first website you look at or view in the morning?
  8. What is one of your unique or quirky habits?
  9. What do you collect? Why do you collect? Have you ever been a collector of anything?
  10. What is the job that you have worked on but never want to work on again
  11. If you could live anywhere in the world for a year, where would it be and why?
  12. If you could live in any period of history or the future, when do you want to live in and why?
  13. If you could replay a period in your past, which period you choose, why?
  14. What’s your favorite movie
  15. What article you read, why interesting?
  16. If you could have any animal as a pet, what would you have and why?
  17. Describe something interesting about someone in your family.
  18. From past to now, who you want to meet most? Which superstar you want to meet in café
  19. When you have a question, whom do you approach for advice and why?
  20. What are you most passionate about? Why?
  21. Tell us about an organization to which you have dedicated significant time. Why was it meaningful to you?
  22. What will you do tomorrow afternoon?
  23. Your biggest challenge of being a leadership
  24. What you do recently to improve your network
  25. Companies should rely on inside experience employees instead of outside experts. Agree?
  26. Who is your personal hero and why?
  27. What would you do if you have one year off from work?
  28. What's your favorite activity in the community?
  29. Who is your favorite teacher, and what makes him/her special?
  30. Who is the most influential manager or leader in your career?
  31. If you had an extra hour every day, what would you do with it?
  32. If money were not a concern, what would you do? = What would you do if you won the lottery?
  33. What is your special talent?
  34. What wakes up you in the morning every day
  35. Interesting thing you do outside class
  36. Your favorite color?
  37. What would be a thing that you wish to eliminate from your daily schedule?

  38. The second question will be an opportunity to describe your plans for the future and how Kellogg will help you on that journey. “What path are you interested in pursuing, how will you get there, and why is this program right for you?” （career path + Why Kellogg）

  39. The other questions will be randomly generated questions that will be similar to interview questions. “Tell me about a time you were not going to be able to meet a deadline. How did you handle the situation? What was the outcome?”

  40. Give me an example when you step out of your comfort zone and what do you learns? Or how do you feel?
  41. Give me an example when you have to alter your existed method and what do you learn from that
  42. A time in a team when you were assigned a work that you didn’t want to do. How did you behave? What was the outcome?
  43. Describe your one adventure and what you learned? = A experience to take risk
  44. You design a target but cannot achieve it. Feeling?
  45. Tell me about an experience that you have diversified your academic or social environment at your undergraduate institution
  46. Talk about a team working experience when thoughts between the team members could not be settled. What is the outcome?
  47. Describe an environment make you thrive. What elements make you thrive?
  48. Describe a dysfunctional teamwork and how you solved it.
  49. What's your biggest achievement?
  50. The time you got wrapped up by something you lost time track? How does it feel? Why? （描述你全神贯注于某件事而忘记时间是什么时候？ 感觉如何？ 为什么会有这种感觉？ ）
  51. The time you became overwhelmed? How do you feel? How do you response?
  52. What motivates you to work hard?
  53. What's favorite thing about your career?
  54. What’s your most useful feedback you have received
  55. Give an example on how you used your networks to solve something/achieve something/help someone
