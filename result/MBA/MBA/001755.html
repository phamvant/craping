EDHEC BM 面经 [2018.04.01]

有一道没见过的题：

How do you learn new concepts? Tell us about your own learning methodology.

其他都是之前面经里的题，就不说了。 我感觉自己没发挥好，看了下面试视频，眼睛没地方放到处瞟，表情僵硬不阳光。感觉要凉……建议今后打算申请EDHEC的小伙伴多利用练习问题锻炼面部表情，目光尽量固定在镜头上，另外整理了下近年BM的面经，方便查阅

EDHEC BM

  1. Why you are interested in EDHEC and in this particular program?
  2. Why do you consider yourself a good fit for this program?
  3. What would your professor/employer recommend as an area of improvement for you?
  4. What's the best advice you ever received?
  5. What is your personal motto?
  6. What challenges are you looking for in your job?
  7. Do you prefer to work alone or on a team?
  8. What makes a brand stand out with its competitors?
  9. 大致描述是你在一个group中play an important role
  10. A decision that you have put off the longest. Why?
  11. Why should we offer seat for you? What makes you stand out compared with other candidates?
  12. 也是描述你在一个group中遇到什么困难，怎么解决。NB：是描述一个你不是leader的情况。
  13. 如果团队里有个很强的同事，还有个很弱的同事，你会如何跟他们合作。 如果你是leader，你会如何inspire这个team。
  14. Which courses in the core modules of the program attract you most and why.
  15. What are your career goals? Which competencies would you like to develop to achieve your goals?
  16. What do you think are the next big changes and emerging technologies that will disrupt industries over the next five years?
  17. Describe an example of when you worked to clear goals or deadlines. How did you achieve and what's the overall outcome? What is the big decision did you make recently? How did you go about it?
  18. 描述一个你做对了但是别人做错了最后别人不得不接受你的意见的事
  19. What's your passionate about?
  20. 以前没做过的事情?
  21. 学过什么课程让你觉得可以帮助你进入EDHEC的学习?
  22. 有什么关于学校以外的事情帮助你发展一些兴趣或能力，以及什么样的学术经历帮助你shaped a good quality?
  23. 从你的实习或学习经历中获得了什么？(satisfaction)
  24. Experience as a member of the team
  25. 谁对你影响很大
  26. how to get new ideas
  27. 最有成就的事情
  28. 擅长写什么样的文章
  29. 满意的idea 和 project
  30. 怎么收队员. 做了怎么样的准备
  31. 最享受的课外活动
  32. 你可以给EDHEC带来什么
  33. Which business sector interests you most? And why?
  34. Everyone has to bend or break the rules sometimes. What's your experience?
  35. 优秀企业家应该具备哪三个能力
  36. 你影响别人的一件事
  37. 解释你怎么管理解决一个困难问题，用什么方法
  38. When have you risen to a challenge? 描述角色和产出
  39. 你作为成员参加一个项目的经历
  40. 为什么觉得自己qualified？
  41. How do you learn new concepts? Tell us about your own learning methodology.
  42. You now have 2 minutes to add any further comments.
  43. 其他你想让EDHEC知道的
