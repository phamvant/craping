McGill MBA 面试 [2017.02.02]

面试之前在CD找了McGill MBA的题目，感觉非常少。现在刚面完赶紧分享给大家，帮助后来的朋友们。

  1. your working experience
  2. your leadership example; what is the quality of the leader
  3. your volunteer work in Uk
  4. if your colleague does not work, what did you do?
  5. your interest/hobby/your travel in China/Europe
  6. what will you do to meet your career goal in MBA study?
  7. what can you bring to McGill MBA?
  8. your strength and weakness
  9. your short-term and long-term career target 10.any question
