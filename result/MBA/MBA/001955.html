Mcmaster Coop Degroote 面经 [2019.07.03]

Degroote 面经

Character setting： 我给自己设立了一个表现目标让自己的回答尽量靠近这几点

  • Be able and be prepare to find coop and job
  • Good team player and be involved
  • Mood maker

我的面试问题

  1. Why MBA/Degroote
  2. Why you are a strong candidate
  3. An innovation idea at work
  4. Leadership experience
  5. Team work experience

还有一道情景题：我不记得了……、

以下是我准备的面试题

  1. Introduce yourself.
  2. Why mba, why school?
  3. Career plan, how would you prepare for your coop?
  4. Big accomplishment.
  5. A time you nearly miss deadline.
  6. Project that your peer is responsible for errors, what you do?
  7. Teamwork experience.
  8. Experience handling boring work.
  9. A problem you had in your work or study, how you solved it?
  10. Coach others.
  11. 如果在找 coop 的过程中，一直没有收到面试邀请，怎么做？
  12. Fail and learn.(Goal not reached, and lesson learn)
  13. Lead experience.
  14. Anything else they should know.
  15. Conflict and how to solve?( conflict with superior)
  16. What do you do for fun?
  17. Questions.
  18. Why Canada.
