UT Austin MBA面经 [2012-03-01]

我也来贡献一点面经给大家吧，我是在上海和一个校友面的，选了个老外，因为他的专业方向和我一样。校友人很好，问的问题也是常规的，时间45分钟吧。

具体如下：

  1. walk me through your resume, including s-t and l-t goals
  2. why mba, why Austin
  3. leader ship style
  4. the most rewarding thing from your job and most frustrating aspect
  5. your role model
  6. how do your colleagues think of you?
  7. Other school, why?
  8. Two ticket question
  9. Question for him

从CD上收获了很多，也祝大家好运！
