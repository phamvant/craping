加拿大布鲁克 Brock MBA 面经 [2018.04.06]

4.6新鲜面经，回馈CD。面试官是白人男性，没开摄像头，一开始比较严肃，后来比较和蔼，每回答完一个问题会给excellent or good的评价。

问了6个问题，聊了15分钟左右：

  1. why pursue MBA in Brock?
  2. when you are on the way to an important interview,you encountered a child who got injured and nobody was around except you, what would you do?
  3. why choose to study in Canada?4、describe your undergraduate background and how it relates to the Master program that you want to pursue?
  4. your short term and long term career goals?
  5. describe a time when your teammates have conflicts or disagree with you, what would you do ?   
  6. any questions?
