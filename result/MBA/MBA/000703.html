UC Irvine The Paul Merage MBA 面经 [2014-02-26]

交的晚只赶上第三轮

  1. Walk through Resume
  2. 之后按照工作经历一个个提问
  3. Why chose to set up a firm at college？
  4. How did your partners chose you?
  5. Details about this entrepreneurial team and team members?
  6. What is the final goal of the firm?
  7. Why quit?
  8. Quit in good term?
    (我说不知道究竟有没有做到in good term，同时强调我并不后悔)
  9. How did you join a firm in Shanghai?
  10. Exact responsibility in this firm?
  11. Why wanna quit shanghai firm and set up your own firm now?
  12. What is the final goal of this firm?
  13. What will happen to your firm if you go to US?
  14. Apply any other bschool?
  15. Short time goal?
  16. Why merge?How would Merage help you in your future career？
  17. Any question？

面试的妞叫Elisa，语速快得让人发狂……我都被带得加速翻嘴皮子。这么多问题面了不到30分，说的口干舌燥而且语速一块就有磕巴和重复……最后一个问题Why merage只说了一半关于employment data然后咽了口水，结果Elisa以为我说完了直接Any question……

说是会在两到三周回复，估计没戏了……
