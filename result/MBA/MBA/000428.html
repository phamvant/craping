Olin MBA 发面经攒人品，刚面完哈 [2013-04-10]

由于网络不好，面试官Jan Snow 很nice的type了每一个问题，具体如下

  1. Tell me about your job currently
  2. You wrote that you intend to return to the company after your MBA. Are they holding your job for you? What position will you have when we return.
  3. What exactly are your short-term goals--moving the company ahead?
  4. How about long-term--maybe 10 years after the MBA?
  5. Can you give me two examples--one showing your leadership and one showing your teamwork ability.
  6. Why have you decided to get an MBA at this time? Why not work for a while longer?
  7. Can you tell me what qualities or characteristics you have that would make me be happy to be your team member?
  8. Member of the team
  9. Where have you applied?
  10. If you are admitted everywhere, what 3 factors would you consider?
  11. Why Olin?
  12. 针对文书的具体问题
  13. Pronounce your given name.
  14. 我问问题
