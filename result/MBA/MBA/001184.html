Notre Dame & Wisconsin & UIUC MBA Skype interview

两天之内参加三场interview，还都是在重感冒的情况下，真是好忧伤，面经分享给大家。

Notre Dame，面试官Helen，40mins，表情比较严肃，偶尔聊到一些有趣话题的时候会笑一笑：
1. introduce yourself,针对我的一些爱好进一步问了一些问题
2. Why MBA, why now
3. Short- and long-term career goal,do you have some target company
4. difficulty in work
5. if you have a career coach, what knid of characteristics of him you expect
6. Share a fun moment
7. Comments from your leader
8. do you have another goal for yourself
8. Case interview: estimate how many piano tuners in beijing（~5 minpreparation, camera off）
9. Any questions to her


Wisconsin school of business，面试官Elizabeth，30mins，人很美很nice，对简历和essay比较了解：
Your short term and long term career goal, how can you achieve your goal, give some skills you already have
a leadership experience
a teamwork experience, conflicts with others
why mba at this time
Tell me a difficult situation which candemonstrate your leadership
questions to her
anything i want to tell her different from my essay and resume


UIUC，面试官rozy，22mins，人很nice：
感觉和上面两家问的差不多，还要少一些，就是self introduction，why mba，career goal，some experience of leadership and teamwork，只有在最后，她说我要问最后一个问题了，超级严肃哦，然后问我what kind of animal you want to be,真的是很汗啊。。。

希望能有offer，尤其是Wisconsin啊。
