Tepper 面经汇总 [2022.03.11]

Tepper 的 behavioral questions 感觉是问的最全的，为 Tepper 题目准备的例子基本都能应对其他学校的问题。

基本问题：

  1. Walk me through your resume/Tell me about your current role/What is your work week like/How did you come to work for your current company?
  2. What's your short-term goal after MBA/Plan B – Goals/Long- term goal?
  3. Why MBA/Why now/Why our school?
  4. Strength/weakness/hobby.
  5. Clubs or students interactions.

Behavioral Questions:

团队问题 —— 团队贡献

  1. TMAT you helped your team or your company.
  2. TW you were a resource for the company?
  3. TMAT when you have to use your skills to help grow another colleague in the team?
  4. Tell me about a time others have come to you for help at work?
  5. Some learning from an out-of-work experience that you used at work-place.

团队问题 —— 接受意见与妥协

  1. TMAT when you had to compromise on a project.
  2. Tell me a time when you had to listen to an opposite perspective and agree to it?
  3. TMA a feedback you got at work and how did you take it?
  4. The most constructive feedback you got and how you handle it?

团队问题 —— 说服他人/困难决定

  1. TMAT when your team was against you and you hand to convince them.
  2. Unpopular decision? What would like to take back from that decision?
  3. TMA a time when you had to convince your team to do something they didn't favor?
  4. Tell me a time when you had to make a difficult decision and had to take the team's buy-in?
  5. How do you convince the old school guys the importance of analytical abilities in making management decisions?

团队问题 —— 与人相处

  1. Time when I had a difficult time working with some. What did I learn?
  2. Time when I didn't get alone in a group?
  3. When you did something which had an adverse impact on some other team. What was the learning?

工作场景 —— challenge/difficult situation

  1. How you work with limited information?
  2. TMAT when you face a difficult situation

工作场景 —— 工作中学习

  1. A time when I worked on some projects and learnt something unexpected/ non-intended
  2. Time when I had learnt something when working with someone in a group. What did I learned
  3. TMAT when you worked with a diverse set of people and learned something.

工作场景 —— 失败

  1. TW you failed and what did you learn from it.
  2. TMAT when the client had some expectations, and you couldn't meet them.

领导力相关

  1. TMAT when you led a project at work and your leadership was not up to the mark.
  2. Tell me about your leadership style.
  3. Leader you admire.

多样性

  1. TW you experienced a cultural shock. How did you deal with it.
  2. Cross culture experience.

成就

  • TMA an accomplishment you're proud of from/apart from that at work.

其他问题

  1. Tell me about a time when you had to adapt.
  2. What has changed from last year?
  3. Biggest challenge you might face in Tepper.
  4. A time when my supervisor was not very supportive of me and how I handled it.
