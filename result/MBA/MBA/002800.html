WUSTL Olin MBA R2 Interview [2011-12-23]

被第一轮搞怕了，于是申请了第二轮的Olin。

刚面完，早晨五点半起床面试。面试官：Kevin。

面试方式：Blind interview，Skype。

时长：40分钟。早晨五点半起床穿西装真的很傻。

面试问题：
-Career progression
-Different responsibilities of different roles you have experienced
-What are you short term MBA goal
-Long term goal
-Other MBA programs you have applied? What are the differences? (Actually why Olin)
-Whether experienced failure in coordinating the relationships of different people with different cultures and backgrounds
-What kind of roles do you prefer in a team
-What can you bring to Olin community
-Any other questions
