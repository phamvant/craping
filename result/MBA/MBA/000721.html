Emory interview by Aluminus Atlanta [2014-03-01]

CD上得到很多人的帮助，不论是GMAT还是interview,刚刚面完，冒个泡，都是常轨问题。(校友是有一张list of required question to ask)

  1. Go thru your resume
  2. What's your long term goal?
  3. What is your current responsibility?
  4. Why MBA?
  5. What is you current role about? do you manage people?
  6. How can Emory help you to realize your goal and how you can contribute to Emory?
  7. After MBA program, do you want to stay in US?
  8. What is your biggest contribution or impact to current employer?
  9. Question for me?

我感觉校友还是很友善的，美国人。
