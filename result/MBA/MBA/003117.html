Depaul MBA 面经[2012-04-16]

Introduce yourself

Mellisa Booth 是个很Nice的面试官，面试的时候看不到她，但是跟她聊得很嗨皮，我还说如果我去读MBA，要给她画一幅画呢。

  1. Why Depaul?（Why have you chosen to apply to the Kellstadt Graduate School of Business? ）
  2. Why MBA program?( How the program can help you to reach your goal?)
  3. Short-term goal and long-term goal
  4. Internships
  5. What is your strengths and weakness?
  6. What do you do just for Fun?
  7. one of the awards that make you most proud?
  8. Team work?
  9. Conflicts during team work? How do you deal with these conflicts?

这些问题是我面试的时候准备的，自己要熟悉自己说了什么，她会根据你说的问你，她后来还问我，为什么你选择商科，我就说我爸妈都不是从商的，所以我想开拓发展自己，不想从事父母的职业，然后跟她谈到了我的绘画特长问题，总值放松就好。
