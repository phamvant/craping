EDHEC video－interview 新题汇报 [2014-03-30]

之前看帖子说面试的4道题分别是自我介绍，为什么想去EDHEC，what makes you a good candidate to EDHEC还有你想对EDHEC说的；楼主刚刚录了，结果除了最后一道题，前面三题有变，新题如下：

  1. How do you think the program you applied will fit your future career goal?
  2. Describe an important decision you have made in your past.
  3. Give an example a professor that you liked and why?

希望能帮到有需要的同学。

祝好运！
