TAMU MAYS MBA Skype面经 [2018.03.09]

刚面完，鉴于版上MAYS的分享不多，把今天的面试题目分享给各位需要的朋友。

Director面的，Skype，30分钟，人很和蔼，整个过程比较轻松，有点交流的感觉。但是大部分还是自己说。

  1. Talk about sth not in your resume.
  2. Go through resume.
  3. Team work with different opinions.
  4. Innovative experience/ideas.
  5. Sth want admissions to consider.
  6. Weakness and strenghthen.
  7. Other schools have applied.
  8. Any questions? 今天会招75人左右，1/3国际生。
