Interview questions:

  1. Self-introduction
  2. You studied  in UCLA, can you name some differences in study between the east and west? And what's the biggest culture shock did you have?
  3. If your friend is going to the U.S., what advice would you give?
  4. You mentioned that you like entrepreneurs, can you name 3 characteristics of an entrepreneur?
  5. Do you think you have these characteristics?
  6. Name one entrepreneur and give your reasons.
  7. If Steve Jobs is alive and gives you a chance to invent an app, what would you invent?
  8. Why do you want to study in Singapore, rather than China?
  9. Do you know something happening in Singapore?
  10. It's said that the Chinese gov is legalizing XXX, have you heard of that?
  11. Besides what you wrote in the essays, what things are happening (challenges) in Asia?
