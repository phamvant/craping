Kellogg 10/30 on campus interview

Questions asked:
1. Walk  through your resume
2. Why MBA
3. Short term goal
4. Why Kellogg
5. A few words your colleagues would describe you: both positive and negative
6. Greatest accomplishment to your organization
7. How do you plan to contribute to the Kellogg community
8. How do you deliver criticism
9. What kind of leader do you aspire to be
10. Your questions

是16届的学生面试的我，不过她说了不管是谁（AO, alumni, students)面试都一样。
问题没有哪个是意料之外的。

时长30分钟，但是其实应该要45，只是他们想让我去听Dean的 speech才特意提前放我出去。彼时我已经困的眼泪都要流下来了。

祝广大 Kellogg prospective students good luck.
