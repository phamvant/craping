UC Davis MBA R2面经 [2014-01-30]

Round 2 UC Davis Interview

国内本科毕业，直奔美国SF实习，所以就想申请SF附近的。UC Davis每年只招50左右，离硅谷近，可能CD里面不是很多人想去这所。面试通知通过email收到，一对一面试，没有之前的presentation。大概聊了20分钟之后，中国candidate带我吃午饭，下午上上课。

  1. Run through your professioal experience.
  2. Why UC Davis?
  3. What is your post MBA goal?
  4. What contribution you can make to Davis?
  5. Two benefits of your company being with you.
  6. what clubs will you join?
  7. What's your plan on Saturday?
  8. Any questions?
    我问了奖学金，80% 人都会有，但奖金应该不多。我还问了what percent of interviewed applicants get admitted? 有50% - 75%。

好吧。就这些了，还申了berkeley，希望有好结果吧。CD们儿~新年快乐~！愿大家都可以拿到offer!!! with $!!!
