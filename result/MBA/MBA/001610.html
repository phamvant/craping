Foster Skype Interview

General Questions，感谢CD上分享的经验贴，都在里面了。

面试官稍微有点严肃感觉自己有点语无伦次了。

  1. Go through your resume。
  2. The greatest challenge to pursue the MBA。
  3. Why Foster？
  4. Negative feedback/compliments。
  5. Teamwork experiences。
  6. Post MBA goal and how you prepare to make it。
  7. Why MBA？
  8. A time you effected the change or change the process。
  9. Experiences with difficult teammates。
  10. What do you do outside the work？
  11. Any questions？

祝大家面试顺利，finger crossed for everyone，大家都有好结果。
