Duke Fuqua MBA EA Skype面经 [2012-10-17]

由于人在海外，约的是SKYPE interview, 面试官是二年级学生，秘鲁人。人相当的NICE，由于SKYPE有时候语音不是很清楚，面试官还会帮着梳理我的答案。可能由于申的是EA的缘故，问了很多关于DUKE的special rescources。主要问题如下：

  1. Why MBA, Why now
  2. Why now, not next year for MBA education.
  3. If you have the chance to change one point of your career path, what you want to change?
  4. Why Duke
  5. what special programs make you choose Duke other than other business schools?
  6. Any extra-curricular activity you are interested in?
  7. Tell me your leadership style. Give a example.
  8. How you think Fuqua could help improve your leadership?
  9. How you handle the conflict of a team?
  10. How you handle personal conflict of a team?
  11. Share me your proudest achivement personally and professionally.
  12. What motivates you?
  13. What other charaters you think make you successful?
  14. Some points about Resume.
