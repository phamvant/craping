Emory Goizueta & Carlson MBA面经 [2013-01-10]

昨天晚上面的Emory，今天晚上面的Carlson：

Emory

11月底交的package，但是12月底收到通知说没收到托福成绩。。发信去问过了几天又说complete了。。貌似还算R2吧
video面的，面试人是Libby Livingston，很热情很专业

问题都是前辈面经里有的，大概有：

  • why choose your job after graduation
  • what do you enjoy about your current job
  • Why MBA, why Emory
  • How would your colleagues describe you
  • Name some companies and positions you will look for as an intern
  • What concentration in MBA
  • What job you are looking for after graduation
  • What's important in a team
  • additional points you hope to bring to her attention
  • your questions

时间控制得很好，因为我只问了一个问题所以半个小时多点就结束了，说月底放榜

Carlson

也是11月底交的package，电话面的，面试人是Jennifer Fuhrman，也很热情，不过感觉打电话效果的确不如视频，呵呵

问题也没有超过前辈面经的范围，大概有：

  • go through your resume
  • why choose your job after graduation
  • what do you enjoy about your current job
  • What's challenging about your job
  • Why MBA, why Carlson
  • What other schools you apply for
  • your contribution to Carlson
  • what you hope to achieve at Carlson
  • additional points you hope to bring to her attention
  • your questions

时间也控制得不错，预计是45分钟，实际大概40分钟出头吧，说2月15号放榜

感觉这么短时间想展现方方面面不太可能，最好想好想讲的story和points，面试的时候别紧张就行了。祝大家好运！
