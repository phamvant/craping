USC Marshall MBA面试刚结束 [2014-02-28]

面试官是Ashley，问题都是常规，根据我的回答见缝插针地提extra questions

没有过多寒暄，直入主题，30分钟

  1. Go through the resume, talk about your professional experience first
  2. Why switch to the new company
  3. Why choose the specialty for Bachelor
  4. Why choose to move to another city for Master's study
  5. ST/LT goal
  6. Why Marshall
  7. Most important accomplishment
  8. Three words that your friends will describe you
  9. Any hobbies after work
  10. Have you ever been to Los Angeles?
  11. Any questions?
