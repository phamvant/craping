U of Miami MBA 面试

SKYPE Interview  30min

1. why do you choose  你所选择的专业 as your concerntration?
2. What do you want to do in the summer term/vacation(这段时间没有课程，而且时间较长)？
  （面试官说 国际留学生很难在美国当地找到实习的机会，所以，最好说是回国找实习机会）
3. what kind of MBA education do you expect?
4. What contributions can you bring to the class?
5. Talk about your recent work. challenge? specific problem? solution?
6. Any other questions？

面试官很温和，很有耐心。
