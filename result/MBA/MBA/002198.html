Michigan Ross MBA 面经 [2019.12.24]

形式：online interview, 对方为二年级学生，持续约 35-40 分钟。
整体感受：对方比较平和，全程笔记，在某个细节上的问题追问了两三次。
反思及建议：所有的答案一定别给自己挖坑，improve, enhance 等等要有背后的故事做支撑。楼主就是一度被问懵了。

问题：

  1. Go through Resume
  2. Why MBA/school/now
  3. Career Goal
  4. Proudest achievement and learning
  5. Project finished without experience
  6. Ideas or initiatives that influence others
  7. Work finished in an intense deadline
  8. Your contribution to Ross
  9. Questions for me
