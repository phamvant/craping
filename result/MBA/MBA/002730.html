Copenhagen Business School MBA电话面试

昨天晚上和CBS的电话面试了一下，反正是不需要申请费的，所以就申了。面试的是Michala Roder。非常的nice，就像和一个老朋友聊天，基本都挺顺的。

-  Go thorough your CV.  What did you do and how did you come to where you are right now?
-  How you choose business school? Any specific step?
-  Why do you think it's the right time for you to get MBA?
-  What do you think is the biggest obstacle for international student to study in Foreign country?

我问了一些她关于学校的问题，及学校帮助国际学生适应环境等问题。

基本电话面试了50分钟，希望对大家有用~~
