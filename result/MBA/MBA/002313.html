Brock MBA 面经整理 [2021.03.07]

面试前在论坛、贴吧和中介那里找了很多题目，这次面试全中，所以来论坛反馈。整理的面经也是借鉴了论坛里其他大神的分类方法，在此感谢大神们的整理。

应该是非常全面的面经了，包含了 practice 里的题目，因为我不知道 practice 的题目会不会考，但是目前收集到的材料应该是没有考的，但以防万一我还是都准备了。 论坛里其他贴主提到的遇到不会的题目可以断网重进这个事，我没有做过我不知道能不能行得通，然后我考试是选择了分屏，将准备的答案放在屏幕右边，考试的页面在屏幕的左边。如果有像我一样考试焦虑的同学可以试试这样做，能够增加信心吧，毕竟叫我看到问题后临时想答案我还是会很抓马的。

希望能给未来也面试这所学校的同学提供帮助，也算给自己攒攒人品啦，如果收到 offer，会来更新， 再次谢谢论坛其他小伙伴的帖子！希望有机会一起上下学！

面经整理：

个人方面

  1. Introduce yourself? Go through your resume?
  2. Why 留学？Why 加拿大? Why MBA? Why Now? Why you? Why the Brock University (留学）
  3. 你对布鲁克大学和这个专业有什么了解吗？
  4. Do you want to work in Canada after graduation?
  5. Why/How could you contribute to our community?
  6. Can you describe your weakness?你能描述一下你的缺点吗？
  7. Do you have an idol?
  8. Could you talk about your hometown and family? What are your parents do?
  9. How do you know about Brock University? How you apply for the MBA(ISP)?
  10. What do you expect to learn besides from school?
  11. Can you tell me what will bring it to you if you finish your study about business administration?你能告诉我如果你完成工商管理的学习，你会有什么收获吗？
  12. What do you think the teaching philosophy of our university? Do you agree?你认为我们大学的教学理念是什么，你同意吗？
  13. Do you think you can deal with the problems of learning in Brock Universities?.你认为你能处理在布鲁克大学学习的问题吗？
  14. What is the most important thing for you to learn in Canada?你在加拿大最需要学习的是什么？
  15. 想申请 MBA 的哪一个方向？为什么要选择这个方向？你认为学习 MBA 最终要的因素是什么？上课期间老师提问的时候，你会积极回答吗？
  16. 在校期间是否参加过什么活动?是否有任何工作经验？老板认为你的缺点是什么？
  17. 说自己的优点。

全球化/文化冲突/交友

  1. 如何理解 Goodman 商学院 "global awareness"的重要性？
  2. 你和来自不同国家的人一起学习或工作的一段时间。通过这些互动，你能学到了什么？
  3. Can you tell me the pros and cons of learning with students coming from different countries all over the world?你能告诉我和来自世界不同国家的学生一起学习的利与弊吗？
  4. If a group of students move to another country, they only interact with the student among at the group. What advice would you give them?
  5. What do you think is the reason students who travel abroad only form relationships with the people from the same country? Do you think it is negative or positive?
  6. 在国外很难交朋友，为什么困难，原因是什么？
  7. An experience about making friends with foreigners.Challenges in this experience?
  8. 你与外籍人士学习或合作的经历 experience working with foreigners.
  9. Tell me about a time when you met with someone from a different country, 你觉得在 build relationship 时有什么困难？
  10. What do you think of the differences between Chinese Universities and Canada Universities?你认为中国的大学和加拿大的大学有什么不同？

学术背景

  1. 介绍自己的学术背景，最喜欢的课程是什么？
  2. If you could do your undergraduate studies again, would you do the same things you did? If not, what would you like to change?（or Your failure）你最想改变 education background 的什么？
  3. What do you think are the best and most challenging aspects of coming to study in Brock next year?为什么选择 Brock,最期待也最具挑战性的因素是什么？Why Brock?Best and most challenging factors?
  4. 说说你的本科教育经历，当时最有挑战的一件事是什么？
  5. How your undergraduate major related to the MBA(ISP) at Brock?
  6. Why do you think you can study in Brock Universities?为什么你认为你可以在布鲁克大学学习？
  7. What encourages you to work hard and perform well academically? Do you think there are differences in what motivates you compared to what motivates your friends or classmates?
  8. 你认为 undergraduate 和 graduate 的学习最大的区别在哪里？最大的挑战是什么？
  9. 你从导师或者上司那里获得的最好的是什么？
  10. 学过统计学吗？你记得统计学里的一些原理和方法吗？
  11. 英语成绩，参加英文测试了吗？
  12. 受教育背景，是否毕业了？将于什么时候毕业，什么学校，什么专业，大学平均成绩是多少？

Goal Career

  1. 你觉得硕士课程会如何帮助你未来的职业生涯，为什么？
  2. 想想你过去的工作经历。你最不喜欢的日常工作是什么，并且不想成为你未来职业生涯的一部分？Think about your past work experiences. What were the daily tasks that you enjoyed the least, and would not want to have as a part of your future career?
  3. 通过你的工作经历，你认为你最喜欢的一项工作，并且会在以后的工作中继续做的工作是什么？
  4. Can you briefly explain your job responsibility?你能简单解释一下你的工作职责吗？
  5. 毕业后在做什么？描述主要工作内容？
  6. What’s the biggest gain from work to now?工作到现在最大的收获是什么？
  7. What is your ideal job in the future?你将来的理想工作是什么？
  8. Do you have career plan after graduation?毕业后你有职业规划吗？
  9. What is your study plan and career plan if you can be admitted by MBA(ISP)
  10. 你计划如何做来一步一步的实现你的职业目标？
  11. 你最成功的一次职场成就 successful professional achievement.
  12. 你不想为什么样的公司工作？为什么？
  13. How can companies attract and retain their best talents?
  14. 未来想工作的 company?
  15. What future careers or fields attract you the most? Why?

团队合作

  1. Graduate programs at Goodman require a substantial amount of group work. Tell me about a time when you were working with a group that wasn't functioning well together. What did you do, and what was the outcome?
  2. What’s your leadership style?
  3. Leadership 的事情作为 leader 的经历，怎么做，outcome?

语言

  1. What's your weakest area in English language? What strategy are you currently using to improve your English language proficiency?
  2. 你学英语有什么 concerns? What strategy are you currently using to improve your English language proficiency?
  3. Can you tell me the importance of English or other languages?你能告诉我英语或其他语言的重要性吗？
  4. 国外学习中，会遇到很多困难，比如语言，文化，在语言提升方面你有什么 好的方法吗？
  5. 是什么激励你在全英文环境中学习，目前你使用什么策略来提高你的英文水平？

开放题

  1. 讲你曾经 stand up for sb or sth 的经历。
  2. 能让人成功的三个办法。
  3. If you were to create a slogan for your life, what would you choose?
  4. Quote or saying that inspires you.
  5. How do you manage stress?
  6. How many universities have applied?
  7. Do you have any questions about us?你对我们有什么问题吗？
  8. 你的优点是什么？你的缺点是什么？
  9. What is the best advice you’ve ever received? What is the worst advice you’ve ever received?
  10. Dream vacation.
  11. 这周发生的事情。
  12. Would you rather solve equations or write essays? Why?
  13. What part of your daily routine do you enjoy the most?
  14. What gift have you received that you will always treasure?
  15. What are you passionate about?15.What do you like to do for fun in your spare time?
  16. What is the one thing you can’t live without?
  17. Are you an early riser or a night owl? Why?
  18. What do you consider the most important event of your life so far?
  19. What is the one thing that you would really like to own? Why?
  20. Favorite teacher.
  21. What was the most memorable year in your life, and why?

作文题

  1. How does social media change the business world in the next five to ten years?
  2. How do you think companies can maintain their reputation given the power of social media?考虑到社交媒体的力量，你认为企业如何才能保持自己的声誉？
  3. How to keep work-life balance?
  4. How to handle unhappy customers.
  5. How to improve the loyalty.忠诚度
  6. 企业如何提高盈利能力？
  7. 公司如何保持和增加竞争力 面对持续的不确定性，企业如何获得成功？
  8. How to manage the resource in company?
  9. How do you think governments should address the lack of economic opportunity and employment for young adults?
  10. Balance Sheet 三要素及其含义，Income Statement 组成要素。
  11. If you have a power to change a problem in the world, what problem you will change? Why?
  12. 你做过的最困难的事情是什么？
  13. 10 年后你想以什么样的方式出名？（相当于 10 年后你想成为什么样的人）
