In @ Kellogg 2Y Full-time MBA [2012-03-20] + Interview Questions [2012-01-04]

Recieved Haas' sponsor $40K and meanwhile the admission from Kellogg. Very lucky.

Copy my background hereof: PKU Law 88/100, Eco 84/100, PRC lawyer 3.5 years, G:730 T:104

Thanks to my interviewer, a very nice alum!

My interview questions:

  1. Why MBA? (I also said my career goals.)
  2. Why now?
  3. Two leadership stories (one in university, another in professional)
  4. Conflict story
  5. Why Kellogg?
  6. My hobbies (why art? Why law? Why running?)
  7. Any questions regarding Kellogg or interviewer?

Collectively lasted over 70 min.  A quite relaxed talk.  It was my first interview, so I didn't prepare a lot.  As my interviewer said: speak from your heart and you will be appreciated.

Good luck to all!
