UCLA Anderson Interview Questions [2018.02.02]

Submitted on 1/5 deadline, got invitation on 1/10, interviewed on 2/2.

Interview type: Skype interview with a second-year student, very friendly.

Questions:

  1. Introduce yourself (With a lot of follow-up questions regarding my career goal).
  2. Where do you want to work post-MBA?
  3. Why MBA?
  4. Why Anderson?
  5. Short-term and Long-term goal.
  6. What’s your strength in a teamwork case?
  7. Tell me an example from your previous work.
  8. When you join a student managed fund or a learning team, you have to work with a team and maybe there’s some situation, how will you deal with it?
  9. As for recruitment, how do you expect Anderson can help you?
  10. Plan B.
  11. How will you sell you ESG fund to investors who only want to earn profit?
  12. How is your current job gonna help you to obtain the skills required to become a fund manager?

Hope it helps. Good luck everyone!
