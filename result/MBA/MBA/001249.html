Schulich Business School MBA 面试经过新鲜出炉 [2016-03-08]

刚刚结束面试，经过如下：

面试的人比约定时间晚了6分钟才加我skype好友，先是打字打了个招呼就开始谈话了：

  1. Why MBA?
  2. Why Schulich?
  3. Tell me something about your leadership?
  4. What could you bring to Schulich?
  5. What's the biggest challenge you met in your work?
  6. Do you have any questions?

He said that the the further notice could be received within a week.
