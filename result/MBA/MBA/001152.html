Yale Video Essay 20

刚做完Yale的 Video Essay，一共三道题，第一道和第三道准备20s，说60s；第二道准备30s，说90s。
   
题目难度不大，为了方便大家准备，楼主就把自己搜集的题库po上来了，基本上就在这个库里面，

大家也不用再去别的帖子里面找了。

为下轮面试攒个rp，大家加油：）

第一题：
1. Why do you decide to pursue an MBA at this point of time?
2. If you have 24 hours without resources limit, what will you do?
3. What do you think the most important characteristic of leadership?
4. What do you see the biggest challenge facing leaders today?

第二题：
1. How would you resolve a conflict? How do you manage conflict between two or more members?Tell us what actions have you taken to reach a resolution to a conflict?Think about a time when you had a conflict with a member of your team. Briefly describe the situation and tell us the steps you took to achieve a resolution.
2. A member of team does not share his part, how to deal with? Describe a situation where a member of your teammate doesn't perform his/her job, what did you do?
3. How do you handle multiple tasks? / Think about a time when you worked on a highly complex work project. Briefly describe the situation, and tell us the steps you took and whether you were able to complete the project.
4. Tell about your experience when you work beyond expectation / Talk about a time you perform beyond expectation
5. 工作中你遇到了什么是超出你的预期的,a situation that exceeds your expectation/Tell me an unexpected obstacle you faced, how would react? Did you overcome it?
6. Tell us about a time when you help others achieve a goal. What steps you have taken and how you help others achieve a goal?
7. Tell me about a time when your value was challenged. What did you react and learn?
8. Describe a situation where you identify a problem or opportunity, what did you do?
9. Work life balance- your pursuit in Personal success will sacrifice your commitment to family?/ Professional success should sacrifice personal life?
10. How to deal with change?
11. 你最近做了什么来提升你的network? / Most important quality to build professional network?
12. Whose leadership style do you admire the most? Why? / 你最admire的leader是谁，为什么？/ The quality of someone that you would like to emulate, why?
13. What did you do to make you successful?

第三题：
1. It has been suggested that whether people accept or reject an idea depends more how it is presented than on the merits of the idea itself. Do you agree or disagree? Why?
2. Motivation is driven by 2 factors, self-interest and fears. Agree or not agree? / Altruism motivated more than self-interest, agree or disagree?
3. Do you agree companies should only rely on internal seasoned seniors' advices rather than hiring outside consultants?
4. The government should have the ability to seize private property – with reasonable compensation to the owner –when it feels the benefit to the public good outweighs private property rights.Do you agree or disagree? Why?
5. Please comment on the following statement: Education is not complete without sciences.
6. Automobile is good invention or not?
7. The shareholder value is primary goal of chief executives?/企业高管最重要的责任是为股东创造价值，是否同意？
8. People remember those who break the rule the most. Do you agree or disagree the statement? Why?
9. Debate exists on whether leader is born or is made, what is your view? Why?
10. 全球化是否减少文化冲突?
11. Do you think goods such as alcohol and tobacco should be taxed more? Why?
12. The essence of education is to serve for workforce market, agree or disagree?
13. Social media communication is good or not?/ 有人说网络社交是一种革新，也有人说网络社交是对于真正对话交流的终结，你的看法是？
14. There are those who believe that technology ultimately serves to separate and alienate people more than it brings them together. Do you agree or disagree? Why?
