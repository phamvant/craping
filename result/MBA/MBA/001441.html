2017 Columbia 哥伦比亚 MSFE 面经 [2017-02-10]

热乎的面经，mfe今年用InterviewStream，新形式，共五个问题，每题两分钟，有一次retry的机会，而且retry之前可以无时限的准备，我会告诉你我每个都看了一遍题，准备好久，然后retry答的么，嘻嘻。

  1. What does Financial Engineering mean to you?
  2. If you were given $10MM, how would you invest it in today's market?
  3. Programming proficiency is very important in the field of FE, can you tell us about programming experience?
  4. How would your peers describe your character?
  5. Do you have any questions about our team?
