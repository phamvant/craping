UBC MBA Interview with Mischa Ragona [2019.04.30]

终于周五收到了 UBC Sauder MBA offer，来分享下面经，供今后申请的弟弟妹妹们参考。

面试

  • 面试官: Mischa Ragona
  • 面试时间: 温哥华时间 4 月 30 日 13:45 （北京时间劳动节凌晨四点多）

面试问题:

  • Give me a brief self-introduction
  • Walk me through your career evolution
  • The fun part of your work
  • The challenging part of your work
  • Why MBA (我有顺带提到自己的 short-term & long-term career goals)
  • List 3 things that you can bring to 2019 MBA
  • List 3 things that you want to take away from MBA
  • What is the most challenging part you consider in your MBA journey
  • Share a failure
  • Give me an example which demonstrate your leadership
  • What attributes make a strong leader
  • Who influenced your leadership style
  • One of the attributes your husband or best friends describe you
  • If one of your teammates are unwilling to work, what will you do
  • A recent book you read and what you learned from it
  • Any outstanding questions you want to ask

时间线

  • 2019.01.26 GMAT
  • 2019.03.11 R3 截止前一天提交申请
  • 2019.04.11 收到面试邀请
  • 2019.04.30 面试
  • 2019.05.10 收到 offer

最后，感觉 UBC Sauder 是个很热情、互助的 community，感谢申请过程中帮助过自己的校友和同学们。
