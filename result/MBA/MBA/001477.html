UW Foster 面经 [2017-02-28]

攒人品迅速来发帖！在北京和Jessica F2F面的。人非常nice，不会特别tough。基本没有什么特别出格的问题，之前的前辈们都分享过的：

  1. tell me about yourself
  2. career plan; why mba (my answer is consulting)
  3. why do you want to be a consultant
  4. what capabilities do you think a good consultant needs
  5. tell me about an experience you receive feedback
  6. what's the biggest accomplishment(professionally, and in your personal life)
  7. what are you passionate about
  8. what do you want to ask me

很常规，很轻松。全程40分钟，聊得很开心。

Foster是我的top choice，非常非常希望可以去！也祝其他面试的伙伴顺利！
