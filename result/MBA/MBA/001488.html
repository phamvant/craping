Brock University MBA 录取 +面经（本地班） [2017.02.23]

2.28录取的
2.23面试: 面试官很凶，在challenge 我雅思。我写作口语只有6

  1. How do you know goodman business school? How do you think MBA program can help you achieve your career goal

  2. Do you know St. Catherine (学校所在城市）

  3. 奇怪问题来了： Why do you want to take the 2-year program? Do you want to take MPAC program?
    我懵比了。因为他读的就是M-pac 。。。“What is the MPAC?" "It is a brand-new program,. It lasts for 9 months....."
    奇怪了。。。。”I have no idea about that..." (开始生气了）:" It is a brand new!! You just answer YES or NO?"
    我回答NO

  4. You only got IELTS 7.5...Do you know you just met the minimum requirement?
    答：忙别的。我以为达到就可以了。另外，我专业是全英语教学blabla,这两年有提高。

  5. Why don't you take IELTS once again? Do you know oral and writing English are very important?
    我无语。。重复多一次回答

后面的问题是 讲讲我的专业，课外活动，实习。最后让我问他问题。其中我问他国际生怎么准备在加拿大的学习和career 他只说English......

唉，只有一个学校录取。。。不确定去不去。也求多点关于Brock的信息。
