2020 Fall UT Austin McCombs MBA R2 面经 [2020.02.06]

面试是一位MBA在读2年级学生面的。 据他所说只看过我resume应该就是blind interview了。面试时间30分钟。

  1. Walk me through your resume/Introduce yourself
  2. Why McCombs?
  3. Short- and long-term goals?
  4. Why now? Why is now the right time to get an MBA?
  5. What is your plan B if these goals don’t work out?
  6. What types of activities will you be involved with at McCombs?
  7. What’s your hobby? What are your passions for outside work?
  8. What's your challenge outside professional work?
  9. What did you learn through the application process?
  10. When was a time you received feedback or criticism?
  11. Tell me about a time you get others involved? （大概是这个意思）
  12. Where else are you applying and where do you rank McCombs?
  13. What are your strengths and weaknesses?
  14. Q&A.

Timeline: 1/7 提交申请，1/21面试邀请，2/6面试。

祝大家都拿到心仪的offer！
