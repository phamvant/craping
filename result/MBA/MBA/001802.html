Yale SOM 2019 R1 on campus 面经 [2018.11.13]

Yale 

面试是on campus，跟二年级学生面的。

30分钟。他也是有一张Form，上面有问题，整个Visit最有价值的是Dean Snyder的演讲, 会让你思考MBA的价值。

  1. Tell me about your work experience
  2. Tell me about a time you failed
  3. Tell me about an achievement that means most significant to you
  4. Why MBA
  5. Career goal post MBA
  6. How will you contribute to the class
  7. Why did you choose the quote （拿到面试后SOM会让你submit quote）
  8. Anything you want to add

Yale的Decision要12月才知道
