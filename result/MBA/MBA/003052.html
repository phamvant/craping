NTU 南洋理工 MBA 面经 [2012-3-07]

面得一般，开始时感觉还ok的，问到一个没准备的问题就开始卡了。面试官是个泰国人，不过在美国待过很多年，所以基本上没有口音问题。

Interview questions:

  1. Self-introduction
  2. Why MBA?
  3. Why now?
  4. Why quit your job to apply, instead of applying at work?
  5. Why Singapore?
  6. Why do you want work in finance?
  7. What do you think of the lower GDP expectation (7.5%) of China in 2012?
  8. Assess of China's economy?
  9. Talk about your overseas experience.
  10. Assessment of American people.
  11. Did you apply for other MBA programs in Singapore?
  12. What is the difference between NTU and NUS?
  13. What's you contribution to NTU class?
