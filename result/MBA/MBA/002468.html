HKUST MBA 2023 面经 [2022.10.28]

最近刚面完，发些问到的 general 点的问题攒攒人品，希望能有个好结果。面试很大程度基于你的过往经历和你面试中提到的内容展开。

Interview Questions:

  1. Self-introduction and why HKUST’s MBA?
  2. What do you think you should improve through HKUST’s MBA program?
  3. Do you have direct sub-ordinates to lead? What’s your leading style?
  4. Except from taking MBA courses,what else have you done to improve your skills?
  5. How to solve conflicts between different teams?
  6. What are your interesting classes in HKUST’s MBA?
  7. Ask back.
