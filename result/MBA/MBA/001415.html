USC Marshall MBA 面经 with Elaine [2017-01-19]

刚刚面完USC，西部的时差真是醉人，面试官是Elaine Padilla, 一位略微高冷的面试官，网络很差我问她能不能关掉视频，她果断拒绝了。。。

整个面试过程都是围绕简历的，问题如下：

  1. Where is your university located?
  2. What do you most interested about finance?
  3. Why go study overseas?
  4. Part-time job during master study
  5. Why KPMG, How do you find your job in KPMG?
  6. Why change job to another company?
  7. Why MBA?
  8. Why Marshall?
  9. Which class do you most interested to take in Marshall?（这题完全没准备啊）
  10. What are your goals? Stay in US or go back to China or other countries after graduation?
  11. What role do you play in a team?
  12. What contributions can you bring to the class?
  13. What was your last travelling experience?
  14. Why city do you like best? 15.Q&A

Behavior Questions几乎一个没问，如果是同一个面试官的童鞋们请好好准备简历相关的问题, 很多follow-up questions，但整体气氛很像聊天，good luck to you all~~~
