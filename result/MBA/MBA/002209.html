Kellogg Full Time MBA 面经 [2020.10.06]

刚刚结束面试就来向各位汇报！ 节奏相当紧张，全程没有太多表情，后来 Q&A 的时候才感觉亲切一些。面试官不愧是 MBB 搞咨询的，相当犀利，不给我喘息的机会。

希望各位都有好的收获！加油！

时间：1 小时 面试官： Year 2 student

问题如下：

  1. Walk me through your resume.
  2. What does your company do?
  3. How did you find yourself working with people with different background?
  4. How was your career trajectory different from your peers?
  5. What is a piece of constructive feedback you received and how did you react?
  6. Tell me how others would describe you?
  7. Why MBA?
  8. Why Kellogg?
  9. Short term and long-term career goal.
  10. Your biggest achievement within the company.
  11. What are the reasons that made your achievement happen?
  12. Walk me through your typical day at work.
  13. Tell me a time when you pick yourself up from difficult situations
