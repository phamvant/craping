Booth 面经 [2018.01.28]

Booth Interview（R2，Waitlist后ding）
幸运地遇到了一位非常nice的美女校友面试官，上来就说我过会会go through一些标准问题，完全没有再多加发挥

  1. Self introduction.
  2. If asking your friend to use three words to describe you, what would that be?
  3. Any example that you got your ideas accepted by others.
  4. Any failure or tough period.
  5. Why MBA?
  6. Career Goals.
  7. Why Now?
  8. How can Booth help you?
  9. Any more you want to share?（这里开始切到中文了）
  10. Any question for me?

中文又聊了大概10分钟，非常open地分享了她自己的职业经历，以及Booth的经历是如何在Biz knowledge和mindset两个层面都帮助到了她。真的是非常authentic的一个校友, 感觉Booth在我心中的印象分蹭蹭地又上涨了~
