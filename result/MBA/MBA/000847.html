面试题目很常规 挺轻松的。

  1. Why choose to pursue MBA now (given 2 years of working experience vs. average of 5 years)
  2. Teamwork (how to approach team work; when someone is not doing his/her share)
  3. Leadership (my leadership style; example of leadership)
  4. Worst decision made and what I could have done differently
  5. Example of a creative approach
  6. One word to describe myself
