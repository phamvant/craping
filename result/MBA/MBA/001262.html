分享一下题目，攒些人品

1.Describe a recent situation in which you were working with others on a project, and these co-workers seemed not to be giving their best efforts. What did you do? What were the outcomes? What would you do differently if faced with a similar situation?

2.Provide an example of an especially influential/impactful presentation that you delivered. What were the circumstances, what did you do to prepare and execute and what did you learn from the experience?

3.Share a recent example of how you engaged others to participate in community activities outside of the workplace. Please include specifics on your actions, the outcomes and lessons learned.
