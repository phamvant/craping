Sloan MBA 面试问题汇总 [2013-03-05]

自己这周五有MIT MSMS的面试，sloan说跟MBA面试一样，都是BEI。鉴于本人工作经验有限，为了准备BEI而总结了一下CD上和clearadmit上过去的面经，好仔细想想怎么白活，详见下文和附件。 可以用一个事例回答的问题我都分在了一组，不过面经里都说一个问题最好准备几个例子，因为有时候讲完一个例子面试官还会再问有没有其它例子。

自己费了半天劲分类总结的只自己用就浪费了 希望能帮到其他人

所有问题都从CD和clearadmit (http://blog.clearadmit.com/category/interview-reports/school/mit-sloan/ )上摘录。

  1. Anything new to add to your resume since you submitted your application?
  2. What are you doing at work these days?
  3. Tell me more about your current responsibilities.
  4. What item would you like to highlight from your resume?
  5. What experiences would you like highlight from your resume?
  6. Tell me about a time when you had to do something that you didn’t want to do.
  7. What surprises you most about MIT?
  8. Tell me about a project at work that you co-managed.
  9. Tell me about a significant leadership experience.
  10. Tell me about a project management experience you’ve had.
  11. Tell me about a time when a teammate wasn’t pulling his/her weight, what did you do?
  12. Describe a time you had to work with a difficult person.
  13. How did you manage to resolve a conflict situation and move the team forward?
  14. Tell me about a time you led a team to a solution.
  15. Tell me about a time when you were part of a team that had poor dynamics/didn’t get along well.
  16. Describe a time when you’ve confronted an obstacle.
  17. Tell me about a time you worked with someone you didn’t want to work with.
  18. Tell me about someone you had difficulty communicate with.
  19. Tell me about a time where you had an impact on a group.
  20. Tell me about a time you received feedback from a supervisor. How did you feel and what did you think when he sat you down to talk? Have you received any feedback from your current supervisor?
  21. Tell me about constructive feedback you’ve received.
  22. Describe a time you had to take feedback and what was your response to it.
  23. Tell me a time when you influenced someone.
  24. Give me an example of a time you had to mentor someone.
  25. Tell me about a mentor or a person in your life who has influenced you. Describe that influence.
  26. Describe a time when someone’s input impacted your actions.
  27. Tell me about a recent setback you’ve encountered at your current position. What happened, how did you feel, and what did you do?
  28. Talk about a time when you had a difficult time with your job?
  29. Talk about a difficult situation at your current employer and how you navigated it.
  30. What surprised you at your current employer?
  31. Tell me about a time you helped a colleague who was struggling professionally.
  32. Tell me about a time you had to persuade your colleagues.
  33. Give me an example of a time you had to convince someone.
  34. Tell me about a time when you had to convince someone to follow your plan.
  35. Tell me about a time you had to sell an idea.
  36. An example of when your ideas were not accepted.
  37. Tell me about a time you butted heads with someone.
  38. Tell me about a time when you butted heads with a co-worker/client/employee.
  39. Tell me about a time when you had to assert yourself where people may not have been receptive of your idea.
  40. Tell me about a time you had to convince others to see your perspective.
  41. Tell me about a time your idea was rejected.
  42. Talk about a time when you were managing someone and they didn’t meet my expectations.
  43. Tell me about a time someone failed to meet your expectations.
  44. Tell me about a time you had a challenging interaction with someone.
  45. What would your recent team members say about you, if they were asked what it is like to work with you?
  46. How would a friend describe you? A client?
  47. What is the coolest thing that has happened to you in the past year?
  48. Tell me about a time when you had to decide between many different routes or options.
  49. Describe a situation when you had to adapt to another person.
  50. Give me a short description of a recent accomplishment that’s not in your application.
  51. Give me a short description of a recent accomplishment at work
  52. Tell me about your biggest accomplishment.
  53. Tell me about a latest accomplishment since you submitted application.
  54. Tell me about a project at work within the last year that you’re proud of.
  55. Give an example of a time when your actions annoyed a superior or colleague.
  56. Tell me a time when you thought outside of the box.
  57. Tell me when you did something innovative.
  58. What great experience have you had on your job within the last one year?
  59. Tell me about a time where you had to make two groups come to a consensus.
  60. Tell me when you had to resolve a conflict between two people.
  61. What are some personal and professional goals you have set for yourself?
  62. Give me a personal goal you have set or are working on.
  63. What’s a personal goal that you’ve set for yourself recently?
  64. Tell me about a time when someone needed your help.
  65. Tell me about a time you had to ask for help.
  66. Tell me about a time when you were overwhelmed and asked others for help.
  67. Tell me of a time when you set up a goal and moved towards achieving it
  68. Tell me a time when something unexpected happened to you.
  69. Tell me about a time when you had to step out from your comfort zone.
  70. Tell me about something that you’ve encountered, at work or outside of work, that made you feel uncomfortable.
  71. Give me an example of when you failed at something.
  72. Tell me about a time when you tried something new and how it turned out?
  73. Tell me a time when you felt your values were challenged.
  74. Tell me about a situation where you decided to take risks.
  75. Tell me of a time when you took the risk and the outcome, what did you learn from it?
  76. What do you do outside of work?
  77. What are your hobbies?
  78. What would you like me to communicate to the admission committee about you?
  79. Is there something you wish I had asked?
  80. What else do you want me to know?
