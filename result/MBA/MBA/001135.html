IN@Wharton、Hass，附MIT、Wharton、Haas、CBS面经

Background
?北美本科, GMAT: 730, GPA: 3.39, Software Engineer/ Startup founder
?工作經驗: 申請時 26 Months, 38 Months @ Matriculation


MIT Sloan (LA/ Off-campus/ AdCom/ Waitlist)
1.Any update in your application?
2.What happens if you leave your startup for MBA?
3.Why Sloan/ MBA now?
4.A couple behavioural questions.
5.What's your leadership style?
6.Do you have any personal goal outside of work?
7.One question for me.
8.整體感覺很好, feedback也很好, 不知道為何還被Waitlist.


Wharton (Philly/ On-campus/ Current Student/ TBD 45 mins/ One-on-one 15 mins/ Admit)
1.TBD - 個人認為主要是看團隊合作跟leadership traits而不是真正討論出結果. 我們那組的面試官在講解時沒講清楚要在碼錶響起前留五分鐘summarize, 大家以為是碼錶響起後五分鐘, 最後當我們要報告時面試官說我們時間已經超過了, 不給報
2.One-on-one - 跟我的面試官聊得很來, 基本上問了我很多問題, 但是沒有花時間問我What you think about TBD. 從一開始就問為何選擇創業, 為何選擇Social Entrepreneurship, 面試官自己本身也是走這條路的所以跟她聊了很多跟學校關的program.
3.How to take advantage of school resources?
4.What clubs/ activities do you want to join?
5.Why Wharton/ Why MBA now?
6.個人覺得超過十分鐘, 聊了一陣子發現超時了就趕快結束. 整體感覺hit it off pretty well.
7.這邊提醒一下, Wharton on-campus一定是二年級學生 (Wharton Fellows), off-campus是AdCom, 可以看個人偏好選地點.


Berkeley Haas (LA/ Off-campus/ Alum/ 40 mins/ Admit)
1.基本校友面, 所有基本題型沒有特別難
2.Walk me through your resume.
3.Why Haas/ MBA now?
4.Talk about my startup experience.
5.What value can you bring to Haas?
6.What's the lastest book your read?
7.感覺很好, 她有說會推薦我, 有問題可以隨時reach out.


Columbia RD (Taipei/ Off-campus/ Alum/ 80 mins/ Reject)
1.基本校友面, 但他一直在跟我聊天, 基本沒問什麼, 沒寫什麼Notes.
2.個人感覺不好, 覺得只是純粹聊天應付, 而不是真的try to learn about me.
3.Walk me through your resume.
4.Why Collumbia/ MBA now?
5.剩下一個多小時都在聊天.
6.面試感覺很怪, 聊了很久但覺得說不上好壞, 果然沒多久就被ding了.


NYU Stern (不打算面了)
