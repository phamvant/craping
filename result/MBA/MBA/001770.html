Fuqua Open House 面经 [2018.09.27]

Fuqua的课堂确实团队合作很多，这个学校有着强烈的collaborativeenvironment。

我是主动预约的面试，面试官是二年级学生。面完说不出什么感觉，谈不上多positive，时间也不算长，有点忐忑。申请b school还是抱着佛系一点的心态吧，等风来~祝大家好运！

  1. Walk through your resume.
  2. What characters do you think most important for a team member.
  3. Your teamwork experience.
  4. Your strength and weakness.
  5. Describe a failure you had.
  6. How your supervisor describes you.
  7. Cg/ why MBA /why Fuqua.
  8. Describe a time when your supervisor gave you a constructive feedback.
  9. When you were a leader, how did you gave feedback to others.
  10. How can you contribute to Fuqua community.
