Minnesota Carlson MBA面经 [2011-12-21]

我看过了几分钟没打过来，我就直接用Skype打学校的800电话，然后转的。

  1. go through the resume
  2. activities in university
  3. challenge in MBA
  4. team player & leadership
  5. other things you want to tell us
  6. have you been to the US
  7. hobbies and how to recharge
  8. other schools you apply

期间介绍Carlson program比较多，比如就业率很好等。而且说面试只是一部分，要综合看。2月中旬放榜。最后说了句have a good night。。。。
