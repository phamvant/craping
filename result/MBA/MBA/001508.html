UC Berkeley Haas on campus interview [2017.03.15]

I just finished my interview yesterday. Here I wanna share some interview questions I met and hope that will be helpful.I lived in berkeley for 1 year and am currently living in San Francisco. Berkeley is my dream school. I love the culture here.

Campus is beautiful, interviewer is friendly. I had a pretty good opening on my life in bay area. Then we moved to the subjest.

  1. talk something about you
  2. why MBA
  3. why Haas
  4. extracurriculum activities for fun
  5. hobbies
  6. failure experience
  7. understanding about leadership
  8. teamwork experience
  9. curriculum

After the interview section she introduced herself, I happened to know one project she is doing now. We talked something about her and her focus.
At the end I asked some questions about the curriculum.

I love Haas. Wish we all good luck.
