Cornell MBA Interview R1 [2018.11.28]

Interviewed on campus with a very nice second-year MBA student (45 mins).

  1. Please introduct yourself.
  2. How did you balance your work and personal life?
  3. Describe a hard situation you had with your manager or employee.
  4. What's your short/long term goal?
  5. Why consultant?
  6. Why Cornell and Johnson?
  7. Did you talk with any Cornell student or alumni? What did you learn?
  8. What do you like about Ithaca?
  9. Any question for me?

The interview is more conversational. He even refered other MBA students to me after the interview, but I am not sure if it's a good sign. Good luck to you all.
