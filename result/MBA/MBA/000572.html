MIT 面经 [2013-12-22]

MIT: 招生官面试@MIT； 时长：45分钟

问题(指明不能用申请材料里的故事，要用新故事）：

  1. A question about my optional essay
  2. when you left your previous company, how did you feel, how did you communicate with your boss about it
  3. when you started your start-up, how did you feel
  4. what is the relation between you and your client
  5. tell me a time when you had to change your opinion
  6. tell me a teamwork experience
  7. tell me your recent accomplished project
  8. what makes you land in MIT
  9. how do your friends and clients describe you
  10. what do you want me to tell the committee about you
  11. any question for me

Tips

  1. Stanford更多关注why, MIT则更关注how。
  2. 都要求描述很多细节，比如场景，心理活动，做决定的原因和动机等。
  3. 保持诚恳和真实。没有人是完美的，诚恳和真实会让你变得有趣。我们都喜欢和有趣的人做朋友，不是么
  4. 关注对方，把面试变成一次朋友间的交谈。

祝好运！
