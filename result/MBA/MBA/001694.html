Darden 面经 [2018.01.28]

Darden Interview （R2, admitted）
30分钟Blind review，长自我介绍就开场了，中间会打断问问题

  1. Why you chose Sweden for graduate study
  2. Education difference between Europe and China
  3. What products you work on（我见她很有共鸣，多说了些这个品牌的历史，好像跑题，被她拉回来了....）
  4. Role difference between local and global team
  5. Is that your current role
  6. You've got long and diversified working experience, why do you apply for MBA
  7. Where you want to work after graduation
  8. What's your criteria for selecting b-school
  9. How do you know about Darden
  10. Any activities you want to join (in and out of class)
  11. Any question for me
  12. Any thing you prepared but not yet talk about
