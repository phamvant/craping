Tuck 面经 [2018.01.28]

Tuck (Nov. Round, admitted)
面试的是二年级学生，萌帅萌帅的墨西哥人，上来就说面试分三部分，一是关于简历和career，二是behavior，三是question to him。问题大概有：

  1. Walk me through you resume
  2. Talk about your typical day（followup question)
  3. When you call colleagues from other countries, what do you discuss about (followup question)
  4. Why MBA and why now
  5. How Tuck can help on your career goals
  6. Contribution to Tuck
  7. Give an example that your team in conflicts
  8. Tuck's mission is to educate wise leaders to better the world, what's your understanding of a "wise" leader
  9. Any example on this "wise" leadership you have demonstrated
  10. Any ethical dilema you've met
  11. Any failure experience
  12. A situation you don't have enough information to make decisions
  13. What you are passionate about
  14. Any community service
  15. Question to him（我问了三个，他回答得特别详细耐心，解释了半天）

一共聊了50分钟出头~
