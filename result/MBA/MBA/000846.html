USC Marshall MBA R1面经 [2014-10-26]

踩着R1死线交的，过了一周就发了invitation

约在上海四季酒店，面试官是Anastasia Figueroa

  1. role in job
  2. how do you like your job in 我现在的公司
  3. what have you done in competition
  4. how do you know marshall, why marshall
  5. what will you do upon the graduation from marshall, what kind of companies will you join
  6. personal interest
  7. QA
  8. anything else you want admission committee to know

整个过程面试官都面带笑容，很轻松，还记录了我的几个问题，说一时无法给你答复，回去发email给你，finger crossed~
