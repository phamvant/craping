Carlson MBA 面经 by Peter Woxland [2013-01-11]

像其他同学说的一样 pete非常非常nice 笑了好多次 而且都不是尴尬的笑 让你很放松

问题：

  1. Introduce yourself(what your current work relate you to your MBA study) and your after graduation plan
  2. why Carlson
  3. why now
  4. long term goal
  5. Your leadership style
  6. How do you communicate with you boss colleague or clients?
  7. Have you ever made a mistake and what did you do about that?
  8. What will you do if the project you lead is in emergence(has difficulties in meeting deadlines)What will you do if unexpected things happened?
  9. Other school you applied
  10. anything else you want to add?my questions

45分钟左右 电话面试 感觉比skype自然点  祝各位Carlson candidates receive offers in the morning and jump out of bed some day
