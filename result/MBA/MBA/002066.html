[已录] INSEAD R4 面经 [2020.02.16]

刚做完 Kira Interview，分享给 CD 的小伙伴们：

  1. When someone comes to you with a problem, what would you do?
  2. What makes you stand out in MBA application?
  3. Do you think ‘When in Rome, do as the Romans do’ is a good advice? Why?
  4. Do you think companies should have social and environmental responsibilities? How?

每题 45 秒准备，1 分钟作答。基本就是临场反应了。如果收到面试，我还会持续更新哒。祝自己好运。
