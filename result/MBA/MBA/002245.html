Toronto Rotman ME MBA Interview 面经 [2020.11.21]

整个申请过程非常顺利，从准备申请文书到面试到收到 offer，总共不到三周。Rummy 人很好，还给文书提了很多建议。

简单背景: 国内一本本科，美国金融硕士，加拿大 6 年工作经验 (4 年某大型银行 + 2 年药企 FPA), CFA 持证人 (Rotman 可以 waive GMAT)

Zoom 面试：跟 Angela 和 Rummy 两个人面的，面试题大部分都是论坛里已经提到过的:

  1. Why MBA? Why now?
  2. Tell me about something that you did outside of your comfort zone?
  3. How do you define yourself as a team player?
  4. Describe a time when you improve a process?
  5. How do you define success in MBA?
  6. Anything else you would like us to know about you? Musical instrument? Hobbies?
  7. Housekeeping questions, such as, Will your employer provide any financial supports for your study?
  8. Any questions for us?

基本就是这样，气氛很轻松，面试后一周就收到 offer 了。希望对大家有帮助! 祝大家好运!
