Minnesota Carlson MBA 面经新鲜出炉 [2011-09-10]

今天在MBA tour Shanghai上面的，11:15-11:55，40分钟左右。

面试官： Christina Holdvogt

面试题目：
1. Go through CV: Short Term Goal & Long Term Goal
2. Why MBA
3. Why Carlson
4. Describe a situtation you disagree with your co-worker
5. Describe a situtaion you worked with your colleagues to get a job done
6. What if your colleagues still don't agree with your suggestion after you present your idea well. You will still pushing on that? Or what you will do?
7. Describe a situtation you have to send a bad news to your supervisor and how you manage that?
8. Any other MBA program you are still applying to?
9. Compared with these programs why you think Carlson is still good for you?Any specific reason?
10. Any other questions you need to know about Carlson Program
11. Will you be still open to other concentrations in MBA programes such as Finance, Enteruptership and so on.

大概就这些，Christina 是Carlson的registerar & advising Manager, 很professional，面试的的时候记了很多的东西，并且还拿了一个钟做时间记录。
希望对大家有用吧
