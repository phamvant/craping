USC Marshall MBA 最新鲜面经一碗 [2014-02-27]

刚刚Ashely 面试, 30分钟，不知是否我语速太快，她问了好多问题啊，常规的问题很少，竟然没有问why MBA, why Marshall。感觉像更像是工作面试，不知道这是不是Marshall 面试风格，感觉天马行空的； 而且，Skype 的声音有时断续，导致有些问题听得不清楚，面完感觉一般。

 

  1. Walk me through your resume
  2. How do you establish  partnership with clients?
  3. How do you attract customers ?
  4. How do you charge your customers ?
  5. Your typical day of work
  6. How do you see your company in the five years time?
  7. How did you expand your business nationwide?How long did it take?
  8. What do you plan to do immediately aftergraduation?
  9. What position you envision to take in your shortterm plan?
  10. What would you do , if you had a chance to start your career all over again ?
  11. What do you do in your spare time?
  12. What is your favorite place you have travelled to ?
  13. Why do you choose your industry?
  14. Any thing we haven’t spoken about that you want me to know ?
  15. Any questions for me?
  16. What time is it now in China?
    (最后闲聊了，呵呵)

可能还不止这些，面试了几个学校，感觉Marshall 的问题最多了

Ashley 挺cute的，邻家大姐感觉，只是为啥你问了这么多问题啊
