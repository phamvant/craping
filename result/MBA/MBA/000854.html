Notre Dame Mendoza b school, MBA interview 面经 [2014-11-05]

5th Nov on campus interview with Neil, associate director.

Back then, I caught a serious cold and could not think straight. But the people there were so nice, Neil even asked me whether I needed to reschedule an interview.

My interview went about 1 hour, partly because I was a really talkative person. And I could tell Notre Dame took the interview very seriously. Because Neil was the interviewer I have ever encountered who make the most notes, and it was the only school that had case study.

The quetions I answered, but not limited to, are

  1. introduce yourself
  2. why mba, why Notre Dame
  3. what is the specific company you want to work for after graduation.
  4. any ethical dilemma
  5. how to deal with conflict at work
  6. case study. expect following questions
  7. questions for him

People in South Bend are extremely nice and inviting. And the school is a unique one. Students who are interested in experiecing cultural differences should pay a visit there.
