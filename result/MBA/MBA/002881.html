UC Berkeley Haas MBA校友面试面经 [2012-02-04]

校友面试，问题挺细致的，而且比较challenging。还好写几个essay以后基本都有答案成竹在胸的。感觉是一个问题表，衡量几个项目，每个项目里有一些问题interviewer可以自己选择。

  1. Go through resume
  2. Why MBA
  3. Why now
  4. Short term goal long term goal
  5. If founding a company any idea now
  6. If not go to B school your alternative plan
  7. Spare time in house
  8. Countries has been to
  9. Most challenging part in this job
  10. Biggest cultural shock when moving to the States
  11. Weakness
  12. Clubs interested at School
  13. what will your role in the club
  14. Spare time what you will do apart from homeworks after enrolled
  15. How to balance life and work
  16. Plan after graduation
  17. Your value to the class
  18. Biz ethics dilemma
  19. Any questions to me

我是刚刚发现我所有的申请材料都写的是Hass 囧
