Ivey MBA10月15号Skype面试 [2013-10-15]

昨天晚上面完了Ivey。发个面筋攒个人品。

面试的是Angela, 是career management的人, 还有一位女士旁听，没有挺清楚是什么职位

  1. Walk Through Your Resume
  2. Tell me about your team work experience?
  3. Tell me about your leadership experience?
  4. What is your biggest shortness and what measures have to taken to overcome the shortnesses?
  5. What consulting firms you want to work for after MBA and what location you want to work at?
  6. What preparation have you done for your career goal?
  7. If you are the CEO of your company, tell me three changes you want to bring to the company.
  8. Do you know what would be the biggest concern for the recruiting panel about you for the program?

差不多就这些了。

Angela很简单爽快，没有什么寒暄，开篇就讲就好了。把自己最好的那一面展现出来。
