Emory MBA上海面经with Kate [2012-02-03]

约11:15，11点到，突然肚子疼-__- ，跑到厕所里蹲了8分钟出来，10分时候看到Kate。寒暄了一下，交换名片，看护照，交简历，讲解面试流程，开面。

问我是第一次mba面试么，说这个面试是谈话性质的，没有明确主题，不用紧张。Kate没看过我的profile，他的同事看过，然后写了comments给她，让她着重了解某些方面，然后她会在谈话中做很多笔记。我的面试好像着重在career上面，leadership什么的都没问。

题目很琐碎，基本上就是先go through CV，问一个题目，然后从题目中再挖题目，想到什么问什么。

  1. Is your hometown the same city you took college
  2. where is your hometown
  3. How did you feel studying in such a different city
  4. Why major A
  5. Why intern C when you majored in A
  6. how did you get your first job? campus recruiment?
  7. Why company B
  8. International exchange experience, learned what
  9. Why mba, why now, what will you do in Emory in order to achieve your goal after graduation
  10. Tell me about your job
  11. What will you do after work, your hobbies
  12. The criteria you choose a B school
  13. What can you contribute to your class
  14. What your classmate would think about you
  15. Challenges when you get into Emory
  16. Do you have a mentor in your company or do you have any entrepreneur that you admire.
  17. If I called your supervisor, in what aspect he thinks you need to improve. 我说一时想不出来她换了个题目，what soft skills do you think you need to improve, not professional skills.
  18. Concentration, what courses you want to learn
  19. Summer internship, what company you want to get in, if you could not get into company in D industry, what other choice
  20. After graduation, you want to stay in the states? Will consider other countries aside from China
  21. Why u name yourself E, I’ve never seen such a name.
  22. Q&A

我们俩语速都比较快，整个面试大概40分钟。面完Kate给我一个小册子，刚才Q&A时候她就拿出来给我做讲解用过。然后说对international student有提前一个月的英文课，bulabula，走之前让我在楼下如果看到下一个面试的告诉她在哪。
