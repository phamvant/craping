UT Austin McCombs MBA interview in San Fran [2014-03-11]

Sorry for the extremely late reply. Interview with Brodie Reynolds on 2/20 ( Associate Director of MBA admissions- he is the guy in charge of international recruiting and operations, also leads the admissions committee.)

My interview was at the hotel lobby of Marriott. I was 10 minutes early, I read his profile before I met him but i was still nervous like a little chick. He went over a little about his background and then proceed with the ordinary questions. The hotel lobby was very loud 时不时还有个小屁孩走过来看 man it was a big distraction! Brodie 还拿起Chapstick 塗了兩下，15 minutes to the interview, Brodie 看了下他的 cell phone. Oh, shoot this is a bad sign. 我的心都涼了!

Anyways,

  1. Walk me through ur resume
  2. Short term/ long term goal
  3. Give me an experience where you lead a change? What was your role?
  4. Qualities of a successful leader? 3 adjectives
  5. Mccombs is a close knit environment, what do you think successful team member must bring?
  6. What contribution can you bring to mccombs?
  7. Why now, why mba?
  8. If you have two pair of tickets, where will u go and who would u take?
  9. Leave about 10 minutes for questions.

Not sure if it means much, but good luck everyone!
