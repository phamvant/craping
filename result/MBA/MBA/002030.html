Kellogg R2 on-campus AO 面试 [2020.01.31]

今天在Global Hub 和 AO（Julie Kennedy） 面试，45分钟卡得很准，conversational talk，笔记两张纸，不知是不是笑面虎。

分享下面试问题：

  1. Warm chat, when came here, how long willstay, bluhbluhbluh……
  2. Walk through resume, explain each roleand working details.
  3. Detail questions on responsibilities of each job.
  4. What is good/bad for your current job?
  5. Describe leadership style.
  6. Strength and weakness.
  7. Tell a time that team member hasdifferent opinion, what did you do, what was the result.
  8. What aspects you want to challengeyourself?
  9. Describe a failure, what did you learn.
  10. What’s your STG,LTG?
  11. Why Kellogg?
  12. What can you contribute to Kellogg on/offclasses?
  13. Questions?
  14. What else you want to tell me.
