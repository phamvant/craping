香港大学HKU MBA 夕阳面 [2012-04-23]

lz背景很一般，没有T，一战G600-，R1申请并交了其他材料，回复说要G上600才给考虑。

苦逼拼命了2个月，二战G到了650+，终于给了个面试。中间有机会与Tom通过电话和email沟通，否则估计面试机会都没。

官方提醒：Please note that this is the last chance of the interview since it’s the last stage of our admissions.

面试非常“高效”，只好用这个词安慰自己了，15min不到

回顾一下问题吧，真的感觉和回忆数学JJ一样啊，最开始只记得3、4个，慢慢可以补充到11个了：

  1. introduce your company.  by Sachin
  2. why HK?  by Sachin
  3. how did you achieve this? one detail in resume.  by Sachin
  4. how do you evaluate XXX (a main competitor of your company)?  by Tom
  5. why MBA this stage?  by Sachin
  6. could you give an example how you use negotiation skills?  by Tom
  7. what's the difference of working culture between the two cities (current and previous)?  by Tom
  8. why do you have this career goal?  by Sachin
  9. why not part-time MBA in my city?  by Sachin
  10. how do you describe yourself?  by Tom
  11. do you have any question?  by Sachin

Hope I can get this last chance!
