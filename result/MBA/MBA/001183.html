Calgary & alberta offer and UBC面经

满篇都是大牛学校，实在不好意思拿出手。楼主申了四所学校，目前面了三所（UBC、卡尔加里、阿尔伯塔），卡尔加里和阿尔伯塔拿到offer，UBC还在等消息。

个人情况如下：GMT:640，雅思7.5，985大学，GPA：80左右，工作6年多，公司类似四大

面试情况如下：

1、阿尔伯塔：提交application第二天收到kira 的pre-interview通知，面得惨不忍睹。然后是面试，问题包括：Resume，Why MBA， Why alberta, come  home or stay in canada, career goal, what's business pressure, biggest challenge in Canada study and how to solve the problem, why accept you。

2、UBC：Why MBA, Why UBC, what do you know about 温哥华，career goal, how you face challenge from other, what would you do if one of your team member in study could not met your expectation, what's your most proud thing in the past, what kinds of future you want to have. describe your favorite leader during your work, what kind of company you want to join in and give a name. how you get your position in your company

3、Calgary：群面，talk about yourself、why mba、why calgary、describe a example in your work(according to your working experience), career goal, how our course would help you to realize your goal. how to be a leader

阿尔伯塔和卡尔加里，好难抉择啊，核心目标是就业，求建议
