据sherry说这波中国只面7个还是10个（忘了，lz悲催的记忆力），所以没面上的同学别伤心，这概率太小了。

扯远了，回到面经，挺传统的问题：

  1. Why MBA, Why now, what made you choose this way (means work) ,how do you plan for your career goal
  2. Achievement in your professional way
  3. The thing you feel disappoint and regret
  4. What kind of job you want to do in the summer internship
  5. The risk of joining MBA
  6. 关于你hobby的东西
