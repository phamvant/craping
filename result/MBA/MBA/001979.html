Stanford 2020 MBA 面经 [2019.11.05]

楼主本周刚面的，以下为新鲜出炉的问题，供大家参考：

  1. Go through resume.
  2. Tell me a time when you stepped out of your job authority? How did you convince them to accept your idea?
  3. Tell me a time you were blocked from achieving your goal?
  4. Tell me a time you pulled resources to launch an initiative? Biggest challenge faced during the time?
  5. Tell me a time you inspired or helped others to develop capabilities?
  6. Why MBA? Why Stanford?
  7. Questions for me?

校友是大陆的创业家，极为 nice，不过中间会有非常多的追问，需要对自己的故事非常熟悉，其中问到一个 inspire others 的楼主没有准备到，中间想了 30 秒，但是校友给了很棒的鼓励，所以整体的氛围都是轻松愉快的，全程都有笑容（我们双方）。希望对大家有帮助，也希望楼主交好运。
