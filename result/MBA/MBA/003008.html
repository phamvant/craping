香港Wharton MBA面试 [2012-03-01]

我3月1日在香港参加了Wharton的面试，主要问题如下

  1. Resume Walk-through (work experience, education backround)
  2. Tell me about a time when you managed a team and it failed
  3. Tell me about a time when you participated in an negotiation
  4. Tell me about a time when you managed someone with much more experience than you
  5. Why MBA
  6. Why Wharton
  7. Questions for the interviewer

面试较快，20分钟左右，而且每个behavourial question后面也没有follow-up questions...
