[已录] Brock University MBA 面经 [2019.02.19]

布鲁克大学是 kira 面试，2 月 19 日上传的 kira 视频，3 月 4 日拿到的 offer。

面试问题如下：

  1. 为什么选择布鲁克？这个项目最吸引你的是什么？
  2. 你认为 undergraduate 和 graduate 的学习最大的区别在哪里？
  3. How confident you are to study in a program completely in English? What strategy are you currently using to improve your English language proficiency?
  4. Graduate programs at Goodman require a substantial amount of group work. Tell me about a time when you were working with a group that wasn't functioning well together. What did you do, and what was the outcome?
  5. global awareness对 good man 商学院至关重要，对你意味着什么，为什么？
  6. What is the best advice you’ve ever received? What is the worst advice you’ve ever received?
  7. 通过你的工作经历，你认为你最喜欢的一项工作，并且会在以后的工作中继续做的工作是什么？

写作题：

How do you think companies can maintain and increase their competitive advantage?
