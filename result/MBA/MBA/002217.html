Duke Fuqua MBA EA 面经 [2020.10.15]

时长 45 min,面试官是二年级的美国女生，语速非常溜，问题非常随意，面完眼前一黑，分享一下攒一波人品！

  1. Take 2-3 minutes to talk anything about yourself I don’t know.
  2. Why MBA and Why now?
  3. Tell me about a time you get a constructive feedback? 有追问。
  4. Tell me about a time you deal with people from different backgrounds?
  5. 由上个问题追问 What do you think is the most challenging and the most rewarding thing when you communicate with them(people from different backgrounds)?
  6. 问了简历上的一个点 how did you lead this team and how the team member describe you?
  7. Tell me about a leader you admire most and the leadership style of her/him.
  8. How do you describe your communication style? Use 3 words.
  9. Why Fuqua?
  10. Any questions for me?
