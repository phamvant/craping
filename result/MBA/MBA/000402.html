Finally! Good luck to everyone in Round 3!

T118, G760, 国内非Top4本科，college senior, internships at top consulting firms, EC in social sector

面经：

面试是在面试官的办公室面的，面试气氛很轻松，聊天形式。

  1. background
  2. post-MBA goals
  3. how an MBA contributes to that
  4. two leadership experiences
  5. how I've changed the lives of others
  6. what kind of leader I am
  7. 最后让我问关于Stanford的问题，并对我的career plan给了一些建议。

时间很短，大概45分钟。
