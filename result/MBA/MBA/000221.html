In@NUS MBA 附面经 [2013-01-07]

今天下午收到email通知我被录取。终于可以放心开始plan被搁置了半年多的旅游计划。

交材料大概是十一月中，面试是12月14号。面试官是Director of Graduate Study（整个面试emotionless）和一个career office的director（全程很high）。

问的问题很基本，没有case study，只是想了解我这个人:

  1. talk about your company, what do you do there? Why do you choose this path after management trainee period?
  2. How many people are under you? How do you assign responsibilities to your subordinates? What's your management style?
  3. Tell me about your family business, why don't you join them?
  4. Tell me about a failure you experienced.
  5. 详细聊了一些company和industry未来的东西
  6. You are so young (25yrs), why do you even think about doing MBA?
  7. Any questions?

个人信息：

武大毕业，后来去新加坡读master。毕业后在新加坡工作至今。G720, T110. Undergrad GPA average. 帮NGO做过事，算是个plus。

其他offer还没结果，但NUS是我第一选择，所以非常满意啦。

希望各位申NUS的也能拿到offer
