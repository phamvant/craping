INSEAD MBA 面经分享

以下是面经1：

Lunch Interview with 男神
1 hour and 15 minutes, half time for interview and half for Q&A

The interviewer has read my materials and understood my background and career goals very well; And the interviewer speaks very pure English without accent, so it was very comfortable to speak with him.

Venue: A very nice restaurant near the interviewer’s office

Questions: (Tips: always give a specific answer, example is more convincing)

  • Why MBA ?
  • Why INSEAD ? Why you were impressed during your campus visit to INSEAD ?
  • How many schools you have applied to ?
  • What will you do if you are not admitted by INSEAD ?
  • Which campus and why ?
  • What do you know about the life at INSEAD ? Will your wife go to France with you or stay in Hong Kong and support your studies ?
  • How do you intend to finance your studies ?
  • How could you contribute to the INSEAD community and your classmates ? What is the decision mechanism of SOE ?
  • Which part of China are you from ?
  • (No behavioral questions)

Advice：

  • look for internship and investment bank opportunities in London INSEAD is a name more recognized in Europe, if you really want international experience, you shall stay in London for two years and then come back to Asia
  • involve your wife in the whole MBA process MBA experience may significantly change the way you think, so ensure to involve your wife in the process so that she can keep in pace with you the life-long alumni network INSEAD provides
  • good idea is important and execution is even more important
  • China's economy and its future: still healthy, but may face problems in 10-20 years, that’s a challenge and opportunity of your generation

以下是面经2：

Coffee Interview with 女神
1 hour 5 minutes, half time for interview and half for Q&A

The interviewer has read my materials but due to irrelevant background, she asked me to introduce my background and my job in details; the interviewer speaks very good English as well.

Venue: A Café at Central

Questions: (may interrupt you when you answered for question 1 and asked questions regarding to what you said)

  • Introduce your job, how did you achieve sales growth in the first job, what did you do to make your products more competitive ? Ask a lot about our product pricing model and those under my control
  • Difference between the two companies you have worked for ? Compare European company with Chinese SOE. Which company you think is more attractive ?
  • Why MBA ?
  • Why INSEAD ? Except what you mentioned about international perspective and diversity, what specific things you want to learn at INSEAD ?
  • Why NOW ?
  • How would you achieve your career goals ?
  • How many schools you have applied to ?
  • How could you contribute to the INSEAD community and your classmates ?
  • Will your wife go to France with you ?
  • What do you think will be your biggest challenges in the 1 year ? Fast speed and family
  • Will you pay a campus visit to Fonty?
  • Any question for me ? / My questions:
  • (No behavioral questions)

Advice：

  • Definitely involve your wife in the whole MBA process MBA experience may significantly change the way you think and see the world, so ensure to involve your wife in the process so that she can keep in pace with you
  • Make the most use of the alumni network INSEAD provides, although MNC are about solution, but alumni at that company will surely make connection easier
  • Because people treated you friendly and helped you, so you want to be nice to people come to you querying about the school
