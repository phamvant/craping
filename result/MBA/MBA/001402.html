记错时间，迟到了十分钟，面试主要问题如下：

  1. go through Resume, why MBA, why Tippie
  2. big failure
  3. something you want to change abour your job or your company
  4. your Plan B for your career goal
  5. How would your colleagues describe you?
  6. Your major strength
  7. Which city would you like to choose for your next job, in US, Shanghai or Hong Kong( based on my own experience)
