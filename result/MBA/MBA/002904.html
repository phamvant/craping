OSU MBA面经 [2012-02-06]

OSU 2/6

About 11p.m. lasted about half an hour.

Telephone interview

Questions review

1. What other schools you’ve applied for?

2. What’s your leadership style? Better give an example.

3. How will the program help you to develop your leadership?

4. the person or some events that influenced you most to choose your career.

5. describe a time when the process is more fulfilling than the goal itself.

6. (not sure about this one) how would you feel if someone holding a different perspective solved your problem?

7. other questions you want to ask.

p.s. the speaker is nice, but she’s talking VERY quickly. After half an hour talking with her, I even sound like an American. J
