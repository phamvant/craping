HBS interview面经 [2014-02-19]

刚刚面完 热乎乎的 在shanghai hub

大概25分钟左右 整体感觉是闲聊 想了解你这个人是什么样的 看你fit不fit, 楼主也不知道她评价你fit与否的标准是啥 反正真心be myself了

问题如下 Good Luck to 楼主把。

  1. you joined XXX company, tell me more about it.
  2. how do you think chinese consumer are different vs. western consumer
  3. how do you sell shampoo at price of 2000 rmb/bottle ?
  4. starbucks in china is more expensive than US, are you surprised that it succeeds?
  5. how does your mkt experience changed you as a consumer ?
  6. why consult after MBA
  7. why MBA at such a young age
  8. you love sailing, why not many travels/international experience before ?
  9. anything you want to share with me ?

然后讲了很多关于PIR要怎么写 建议一个小时搞定 不要花太多时间
