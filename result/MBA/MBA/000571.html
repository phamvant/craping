Stanford 面经 [2013-12-22]

Stanford：校友面试@咖啡馆; 时长：90分钟

问题(按先后顺序排列）：

  1. briefly introduce yourself, why MBA, and why Stanford (这三个问题是连着一句话问出来的）
  2. why did you leave your previous company and start your own company
  3. tell me a challenging experience
  4. tell me a time when you led a team, what was your role
  5. tell me a time when you had to deal with a tough person
  6. anything else you want to talk about
  7. any question for me about Stanford
  8. a question about a social activity in my resume
  9. how is your project going here, what do you do for the current client
  10. do you think you could help more american clients' business in the future
  11. any question for me about the local life
