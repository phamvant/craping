Emory MBA面经@魔都，攒RP！[2012-02-02]

面试官是Kate，很nice，一上来问怎么过来的，花了多少时间，还为老远路apologize万豪酒店38楼景色真不错。。。

交上简历，但我觉得她肯定事先看过简历，没有self introduction，直接一个问题接着一个问：

  1. Why choose major A when enter college?
  2. Why choose internship B while your major is A?
  3. Why company C when you graduated from university?
  4. Some details about Promotion
  5. Tell me about one of your projects in company C
  6. Why jump to company D? How did they find you?
  7. Tell me about your current work in company D
  8. Why MBA
  9. Why Emory (我听成why M&A了...真的很像...blahblah了一通以后她很nice地告诉我听错了...)
  10. Charity Actiivity
  11. Q&A

期间Kate一直在记笔记，很认真细致地说。。。感到自己口语真心烂。。。看到这么多大牛报offer，俺心里那个激动啊，祝所有战友都能有一个满意的结果！
