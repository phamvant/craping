Duke Fuqua MBA 面经 [2021.12.15]

Duke Fuqua: Interview with 2nd year MBA – 45 min

面试都是常规问题：

  1. Walk through the resume.
  2. Why MBA, Why Fuqua?
  3. What can you contribute to school?
  4. Long-term short term goal.
  5. What kind of leader are you?
  6. Constructive feedback you revived.
  7. Biggest Achievement.
  8. Team work experience.
