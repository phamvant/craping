Kellogg MBA 面经 With Alumni [2020.02.19 ]

本人申请 Kellogg MMM Program, Kellogg MBA Program 中的 dual degree program (MBA & Design Innovation)坐标 Boston, face-to-face interview

Alumni: Class of 2015 2-year MBA Program, hedge fund company, commercial real estate fund.

面试过程预计 30 分钟，最后实际接近 50 分钟，主要问题有以下几个：

  1. Why Kellogg? Why the MMM Program?
  2. Why now?
  3. Clubs you are interested? Sports Club?
  4. Question about one real estate modelling project? （涉及他擅长的领域了，幸好有准备）
  5. My short-term career goal. 因为我说了关于 long-term 想要创业，他有追问什么Product I would like to launch?
  6. Campus Visit Kellogg? Talk about new buildings a little bit.
  7. Questions for him.

整个过程 conversational style.最后别忘记发 Thank you email!他非常及时回复了。整个过程非常 Smooth, 期待结果。
