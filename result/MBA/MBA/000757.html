UCLA MBA interview! [2014-03-06]

I tour the campus before my schedule interview. I really like their campus comparing to USC.

Straight forward interview.

  1. Walk over your resume?
  2. Briefly talk about where you implemented the technology in your current company.
  3. experience working for a big defense contractor and went back to Asia, how was that experience?
  4. What contribution can you bring to the Anderson community?
  5. What is your short term career goal after college? And long term?
  6. I think you will be better suited for the FEMBA program?
    (他在hint 我不适合2 yr program 吗？)
  7. How do you deal with a difficult team member? How were you able to convince him?
  8. What is the biggest surprise for you when you move overseas?
  9. Leave open about 10 minutes for questions.

There might be one or two questions I missed. Overall 50 minutes, I should've drink some water before the interview.

Good luck everyone.
