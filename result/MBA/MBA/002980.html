UC Berkeley HAAS MBA R2 on-campus interview [2012-02-25]

和adcom面的，气氛轻松，问题都是围绕我的简历，工作职责，在公司进阶的历程和申请HAAS的初衷，回答基本没有卡壳。

  1. what bring you to our program? (why Haas?)
  2. tell me about your company?
  3. how did you progress within your company (I have been working for the same company for 6 years and got three runs of promotions.)
  4. How many people under your management?
  5. What supervisor would say about you? (strength and weakness)
  6. short-term and long-term goal.
  7. what do you do for fun after work?
  8. your background (born in China and went to US for advanced education...)

还追问了一些职业选择上的，公司架构上的，和选择推荐人方面的问题。她顺便推荐了一下巨贵的EMBA program，我表示兴趣不大。我问了她一些程序上的问题，如可否缩短学习时间等。我申请的是EWMBA。附玉照一张，哈哈~
