Duke Fuqua MBA open interview [2012-10-04]

Duke Fuqua MBA的open interview，on campus，由second year学生来面。

首先觉得fuqua的学生真的非常nice，我们在休息区等的时候，学生如果没有课，就会主动过来和我们聊。基本上有问必答的那种。面试过程也很舒服，没有challenge你，就是一个很随意的talk，然后他们会记录。

因为是open interview，只需要CV就可以了。见到很多的印度申请者，就是特意从印度飞过来面试的。还有日本，哥伦比亚~~~~

1) go through your resume
2) Why Duke
3) short term goal and long term goal
4) leadership experience
5) how to motivate team member
6)  how to build a network
7) what is your motivation to do your current job
8) Any other questions you want to ask me?

面试时间大概在50min，最大的感觉是 be honest, be yourself and be confident.
