Brock MBA 面经 [2020.03.09]

首先，超感谢 CD 上各位前辈的面经，题目大致类似 qwq.

Kira 面试，7 道视频口语题（45 秒准备，90 秒录，因为网络超卡，所以准备时间远远大于 45 秒），1 道写作（10 分钟）

一道口语题会有好几个小问，要控制好时间。

  1. Why Brock?Why Goodman?What you will be excited about in this program?
  2. Introduce your education background. What's your favorite course?
  3. Weakest parts in English?How to improve?
  4. Future career goal?Why?
  5. Group work not function well?What did you do?Outcome?
  6. An experience about making friends with foreigners?Challenges in this experience?
  7. Tell me three ways you know that somebody is going to be successful.
  8. （写作）How do you think companies can maintain and increase their customer loyalty?
