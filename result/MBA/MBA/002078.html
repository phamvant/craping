  2. How to achieve your goal through your network before or in MBA?

  7. Biggest challenge during the program.
  8. Supervisor left, what strategic changes would you make?

  10. How do you stay updated on the current events?
  11. What is a recent business topic that you have been following?
  12. Any question?
