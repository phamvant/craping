Case面试，晚上9:30 Deborah 大妈 [2012-12-18]

额。。面试完一阵紧张算个什么事呀

之前还把面试时间搞错了。。提前了一个小时给人家说。。。无语了

好了。。进入正题。。个人觉得面的题目很多。。累屎人了

  1. why MBA
  2. why case
  3. Describe your leadership style
  4. How woukd you like to improve your leadership skills
  5. What is your short term goal?
  6. Is there a specific role you would like to have?
  7. Do you have a company in mind that you would like to work for?
  8. So you would you like to do an internship in the US and then go back to China immediately after you graduate?
  9. So fifteen years from now I will invite you back to CASE as a keynote speaker.  I will have the opportunity to introduce you.  What will I say?
  10. Tell me about your experiecne working on a team when you were not the leader
  11. What role did you play specifically to help the team?

感觉好多问题都是根据你的答案延伸出来的。。。太抠细节了。。
