In@Georgetown [2012-03-16] 面经＋技术分析

Georgetown 是我的第一个面试，当时只给自己留了一天的时间准备，这一天全都花在研究Georgetown上.

The interview was basically a conversation, except a question about my long-term goal, most of the time I was asked to give examples to behavioral questions.

A fun fact is that after I researched Georgetown, I became excited for their MBA program, and I worked "why Georgetown" into a lot of answers. At one point, the interviewer said, I think Georgetown would be a great school for you. - 不知到是否这是我收到offer，不过没奖的主要原因.

下面技术分析如何通过细节判断是否能拿到offer, 但如果分析错了别砸我。

  1. 面试后，不断提示你，有问题请email 我。 - 表示希望保持联系。
  2. 10号登录网站，发现application status中多了一栏 “deposit”，我心想如果没录取怎么会有deposit 的事那？

就是这些了。祝所有等offer的同学好运！
