Kellogg校友面 [2015-02-05]

刚做完校友面， 对方是自己创业的在多伦多， 一CBC。 聊了大概50分钟，感觉她正经问我问题时间顶多半小时，剩下20分钟都是听她在那眉飞色舞的扯淡，不断“诱导”我发问尤其是问她的business方面的。最后感觉就是聊的很热烈，心里很没底。。。。

心里有印象的问题有以下几个：

 1. Walk me through your resume.
 2. Why Kellogg?
 3. How you differentiate yourself from other candidates.
 4. What's your biggest weakness.
 5. What other schools did you apply.
 6. If you don't get admitted this year, what will you do?
 7. What's the weakest part of your application package.
 8. Why you pick your current career post college?
 9. If your long term goal doesn't work out, what will you do?
 10. Hobbies
 11. What's the biggest risk you've ever taken.

就这些了，希望对大家有帮助。
