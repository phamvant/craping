USC Supply Chain Management Program 面经 [2014-03-01]

周二面了USC的Supply Chain Management Program. 面试者是Brittany Hawkins,是个很nice的African American.

上来寒暄了几句，聊了下最近的天气什么的。下面进入主题

  1. Why MBA?
  2. Why Marshall?
  3. If you are USC Marshall's ambassador or promoter, how are you going to promote Marshall?
  4. What can you contribute to USC?
  5. What is your hobby?

面试持续了大概15分钟，不长，人也很nice. 面完就叫我发Bank Statement和passport copy到办I20的department, 不知道是不是录取我了？嘻嘻　希望是吧。

Good luck to everyone!!
