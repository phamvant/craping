SMU Cox 面经 [2013-12-17]

SMU(Cox)

11月初收到了SMU的面试通知，11月8日早上6点面试，Shanna Davis，非常优雅的一位女士，在面试的过程中会主动和你互动，明显她是读过了你的 Application Package，问题如下：

  1. Why MBA, Why SMU Cox?
  2. Career Goal, how would you achieve these goals?
  3. The firm that you want to join after MBA graduation? Any preparation?
  4. Experience on deal with conflicts in organization?
  5. Leadership examples.
  6. Other MBA program that you applied.
  7. Any other questions.

面试总共持续了约30多分钟.

12月13日收到Admission的Email和Packet，4w/y，约80%的奖，但算来算去，去那边读还差8w美刀,感觉SMU真是太贵了。

For the dream, for 2014.
