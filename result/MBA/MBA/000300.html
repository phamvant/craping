UCLA MBA 面试经验 [2013-02-12]

UCLA Skype interview with Admission team:

  1. go through resume
  2. why MBA
  3. why UCLA
  4. contribution to UCLA
  5. biggest challenge
  6. leadership experience
  7. international experience
  8. how colleagues describes you
  9. short term goal, alternative plan in case short term goal fails
