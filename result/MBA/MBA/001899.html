Kellogg Fall 2019 R2 Alumni Interview [2019.03.07]

Date: March 1st (R2)

Location: local cafe 

总体感觉：人还是挺友好的。问了说 100% applicant 都会收到面试。反正我也进不去，给大家分享，攒个人品了。每年的问题貌似都差不多。Kellogg 非常看重 teamwork and leadership。大家加油！

Questions:

  1. Walk me through your resume 
  2. Why the current job?
  3. Did your expectations meet the reality in your current job? 
  4. What do you know about the clubs at Kellogg? How would you contribute to it? 
  5. Why MBA? 
  6. Why now? 
  7. Why Kellogg?
  8. What is one sentence you hope I include in my review? 
  9. What are your strengths and weaknesses? 
  10. What do you consider a good Kellogg student? What would make you one? 
  11. Tell me about a time when you had to work with a diverse team.
