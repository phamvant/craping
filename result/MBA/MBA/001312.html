多大 Rotman interview with Dimitra [2016-05-17]

刚刚与Rotman面试完，跟我面试的是Dimitra，确实如很多同学说的一样，她很nice，人很好，整个面试大概说了50分钟

废话不多说，先放出来今天的面试问题，大家拿走不谢：

  1. Please walk me through your resume
  2. What did you do during your break? (楼主有3个月的break离上一份工作，因此楼主说了去旅行，并且提示说她如果想听的话，我可以告诉她旅行的感悟和细节）
  3. 由于楼主引导，Dimitra很自然的就说了让我说说我的旅行感悟
  4. Why MBA at this stage?
  5. Why Rotman?
  6. Did you apply to other schools? Dimitra有说如果觉得不方便可以不答
  7. Rotman has very challenge MBA program, could you please tell me the failure you had and how did you handle it?
  8. What's your biggest accomplishement?
  9. Scenario: if you boss tells you to do a web design assignment, you never learn the coding or the tools, how will you start your work and present the outcome?
  10. Scenario: How will you start your conversation if you are in a room of 50 strangers in a professional conference?
  11. Any question?

这就是所有的问题，一直从CD上获益良多，希望这点回馈能够帮到大家，要面试的小伙伴们feel free to抱走，有问题给我留言吧。

楼楼这两天就会知道Rotman的结果，祝我好运吧~
