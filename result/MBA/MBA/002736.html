Columbia (CBS) MBA第二次面试面经 [2011-11-30]

昨天接到了CBS的一封邮件，说是对我的application里有些问题还想double check，于是约在刚才打了电话。是一个adcom打的，自称如果admission committee对申请材料有些疑问的话，会让一个之前没审核过我材料的人看材料然后再跟我联系，大致问的问题都跟申请有关，而没有那些行为面试的问题：

  1. Why did you give up a promotion in China and choose to transfer to Australia?
  2. To achieve your long term goal, why do you think you can’t start right away as a consultant?
  3. If you want to join an investment banks after graduation, where do you focus, in terms of both location and companies?
  4. Any backup plan if you can’t join IB?
  5. How long do you want to work in IB before you want to set up your own consulting firm?
  6. What kind of resources do you want to use at CBS to help you reach your goal?
  7. You don’t have any related experience in finance, how do you think you can find a position in investment banks?
  8. Any questions for me?

大概谈了15分钟，然后说会向admission committee汇报这次谈话的内容，之后做决定。看来还得再继续等几天了，呵呵
