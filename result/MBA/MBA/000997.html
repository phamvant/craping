HKU MBA R3面经 [2015-04-02]

赶上最后一轮HKU,3月22号提交的申请,3月27号收到面试邀请,4月2号中午12点的面试.

中午12点01分Sachin和Tom打进来，简单介绍一下之后就开始正式问问题。

  1. Why go to U of T for Bachelor degree?
  2. Current job in company
  3. What differenciate your current compan product from the market competitors
  4. What do you do to motivate your juniors in work
  5. What did you learn from your direct supervisor
  6. Why MBA, why now, why HKU
  7. Your work should be done by an engineer, why they chose you
  8. How would your friends describe you
  9. Career goal
  10. Why MBA instead of Master in Communication/public relation (因为LZ说想要open一个public relation company in the long run)
  11. What make you feel that you could make a good entrepreneur
  12. Why HK/China track instead of LBS/CBS?
  13. Any questions

全程Sachin和Tom都在点头，听得也很认真。后来问了他两个问题他也很认真回答了。

后来都回答了问题后又问我G分数多少，GMAC成绩似乎还没有发到。

希望有好运=。=好紧张啊
