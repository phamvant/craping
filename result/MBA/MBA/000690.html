Mcmaster admission interview 面经 [2014-02-23]

CD上都没什么面经，发个面经攒点人品，也为明年申请的朋友提供一点经验。

总共来了3个面试官，半个小时presentation介绍学校和项目的情况。接下来是1对1的面试。时间在25分钟左右。

以下是我被问到的问题，仅供参考。面管都很随和, 推荐尽量正装 or semi-formal。有的问题会有衔接, 根据你经历的某个点进行提问。

  1. What do you think about GMAT?
  2. Tell me about yourslef.
  3. Why do you want to study in Canada?
  4. Why Degroote?
  5. What's your career plan?
  6. What do you think about leadership?
  7. Can you offer an example of your leadership?
  8. Use three words that your colleague would use to describe you.
  9. What can you bring to MBA?
  10. What challenge do you think you will have?
  11. What's your biggest achievement?
