Tips about life in duke from alumni 2012.12.10 SH

  1. Always E-mail and send appreciations after you had a nice chat with others on the party. Try to find connection with others to leave them deep and good impression.
  2. Learn how to be a high adaptability person, be confindent under any circumstance, broaden your international vision (Duke will offer many opportunities)
  3. Learn how to understand and influence others, focus on the demand of others while you are requiring any help from him/her.
  4. Leadership is an art of managing people and helping the team achieve the result.
  5. Presenting skill is important, learn how to tell a story well.
  6. Passion, Passion, Passion. Do make contribution before you requiring anything.
  7. Be honest, be sincerely.
