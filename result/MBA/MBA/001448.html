Ryerson 2017 full-time MBA Video面试 [2017-02-13]

看到论坛上Ryerson的面试信息很少，上来分享一下。申的是Ted Rogers 商学院2017 fulltime MBA，项目只有一年。这个项目貌似只有人机面试，就像录video essay一样，没有真人面试的。

时间：30s准备，60s回答，表示完全不够用，说不完。

题目：

  1. Tell us aboutone way in which you are active within your community, outside of school andwork. Why is this important to you?
  2. Tell me abouta time when you mentored or helped another person achieve their goals. What didyou gain from this experience?
  3. Tell mesomething about yourself that gives you a unique perspective. How does thishelp your approach to problem solving?
  4. Would yourather be given direction when starting a project or given free rein? Why?

他家的面试邀请挺奇怪的，我去年12月份提交申请后，发了一个邮件给我，要求我两天内把面试做掉，当时没在意，2月13号我才做的，也不知道能不能行。我发了邮件问他们项目组，还没回复我。
