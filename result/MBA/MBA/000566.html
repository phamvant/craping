感觉一般，面了45分钟左右，说1月初有消息。问题如下：

  1. Explain your gap year during graduation and 1st job.
    (楼主毕业后没有马上工作，考了一次研究生没有考上）
  2. Talk about your career progression. Why do you transfer from previous job to current one?
  3. Talk about your strengths and weaknesses.
  4. Suppose you are the executive officer of your company, what 2 changes you will make?
  5. Which CEO you appreciate most and why?
  6. What is your suggestions to your manager?
  7. What are your core values?
  8. Any quesitons for me?
