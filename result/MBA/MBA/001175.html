新鲜面经 - Georgetown MBA

刚面试完，这是楼主的第一个面试，所有的问题都明白也有回答，可是面试官的反应好像是嫌我说的少，一直要求我再talk in details, talk more，最后越来越紧张了。

看之前面经面试被录取的都有要求写cover letter，可是我没有，估计没戏了！

1. Go through your professional background and personal life
2. Your most achievable project? What you have learnt? What you enjoy the most?
3. Where you have been travelling? 因为我提起时常出国旅行
4. Why MBA and why Georgetown?
5. What constructive feedback you received?
6. In your own opinion, what should you improve?
7. How do you gain international exposures, if not through MBA. 因为我说我缺乏international experience
8. Any questions?

楼主要准备下一个面试了，祝其他即将面试的楼主好运！ 加油！
