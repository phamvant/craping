UCI Interview Questions [2014-02-12]

刚刚结束了和elisa lee的skype面试，和大家分享下她的一些问题。

  1. Walk through resume
  2. Had follow up questions on Internship programs, how did you get it and why didn't you stay
  3. What is your goal after MBA
  4. Why MBA, Why UCI
  5. Ask why you chose your previous school and majors
  6. Ask about current job and current company as what kinds of things you do and what kind of business does company do
  7. What kind of job do you wanna do after MBA? What company is your dream company, detailed ones.
  8. Ask her questions and anything that you want to tell her.

我回答的很快，20多分钟就结束了。因为已经收到其他学校offer了所以也没人太认真准备。她说1-2周内就会有答复了。大家后面有interview的好好准备。
