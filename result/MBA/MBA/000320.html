Wisconsin Madison- MBA Market research方向面筋 [2013-02-20]

今晚10点钟刚刚面完Wisconsin Madison的market research MBA，奉上热腾腾面筋。

这所学校申请的人很少，面筋也不多。因为手上已经拿了两个offer，所以心理压力比较小，面试的挺轻松。

问题如下：

  1. Self introduction on your past working experience
  2. Your short time and long term career goal
  3. There is plenty of difficulty to get working visa for international student, do you have any back-up plan
  4. Your management style as team leader and your working style as team member
  5. Casual topic: Your home town is in North China but you work in South China, how is the difference between this 2 regions
  6. Tell me a difficult situation which can demonstrate your leadership
  7. Name a brand which you think have very strong marketing strategy
    (I use the brand of 小米手机)
  8. Please specific your answer on your short term career goal, any target company or position you want to take
  9. Which 4 word your family and friends will describe you, which different word your colleagues will use to describe.

今年的申请到此基本结束，等到Wisconsin结果出来后会为大家奉上一个申请总结帖，前后2年多时间准备MBA申请，希望对大家有帮助，祝大家好运啦！
