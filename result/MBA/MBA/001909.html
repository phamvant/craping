Dartmouth MBA 面经 [2019.02.04]

Dartmouth MBA (3/14/2019 Waitlisted)

  • 2019.01.07 提交
  • 2019.01.16 接到面试邀请
  • 2019.02.04 Skype 面试 (44 mins) w/ 2nd-year MBA candidate

面经

  • Walk me through your resume
  • Did you wait to get your CPA i.e. worked a couple of years (follow up question)
  • How your role has changed since you were promoted
  • Biggest accomplishment in life
  • One time you acted with integrity
  • One time you stepped outside your comfort zone
  • One time you had a disagreement with your manager, how did you approach it
  • One time you helped someone when there is nothing in it for you
  • New things you are learning
  • Why now
  • Why Tuck, classes you are interested in, student groups/clubs
  • Short-term / long-term goal
  • How do you wanna be remembered
  • Question for the interviewer

Tuck 有 self initiated interview.推荐根据前辈的经验，学校还是比较看重的，当时因为比较匆忙，总觉着自己没准备好，我就放弃了这个机会还是等 invitation。

Tuck 的面试不是我面的最差的一场，但是我觉得最难的一场，没有借口。对方很明显有一份列表，虽说有些问题比较清奇，但失利关键还是我没有做好充足的准备，很多问题就现场想到什么答什么，多半还没说完我就后悔了，拼命去圆，现在想起来都觉得很想撞墙。就"跟人吵架，事后才想起该怎么还嘴"同样感受。能给的经验就是准备 Tuck 的面试范围要稍微扩充一些，免得被打得措手不及。
