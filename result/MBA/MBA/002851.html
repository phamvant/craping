Kellogg MBA新鲜面经 [2012-01-29]

Interviewer is a really really beautiful white female, super nice! The whole interview took an hour.

  1. Walk through your resume(very detail)
  2. A story showing your leadership
  3. A story you facing obstacle
  4. Your leadership style
  5. what type of leadership does your boss have? do you like it?
  6. What  do you envision yourself after 5 years?
  7. Why mba?+Why kellogg?
  8. Other school you applied to and what is the criteria of choosing school eventually?
  9. What kind of activities would you involve when you go to Kellogg?
  10. What unique contribution you will bring to Kellogg?
  11. Ask her question~

I only showed passable performance. God bless me!
