Foster Video Interview题目汇总，目前整理了13题，大家可以一起补充~ [2015-02-10]

不知道video interview是不是今年新出的，以往资料很少，为了方便以后的筒子，我收集了一下实战的问题，供大家参考：

根据大家的信息重新整理了下，希望能帮到大家

1.        Who is your role model and why?
2.        Describe a time when your work was criticized.
3.        What was your biggest achievement outside of work?
4.        What have you learned most in school?
5.        What's the most challenging when working in a team?
6.        How did u choose ur undergraduate major, have u considered other majors? why?
7.        What strategy do u employ when working with difficult people?
8.        If you could start a new venture to solve a major problem what would you choose and why?
9.        What leadership ability do you appreciate in your current direct supervisor and what ability do you wish they would improve on?
10.        Describe your ideal work environment.
11.        If you could be anywhere in the world now, where do you want to go?
12.        Give an example of taking a creative approach to solving a problem. How did you go about it?
13.        Describe a thing that shaped your personality
