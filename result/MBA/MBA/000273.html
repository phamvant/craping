HKUST MBA R2面经 新鲜出炉 [2013-01-30]

20130208 update, 前两天接到email offer with $, 特此汇报一下。感谢CD!

10分钟前刚刚面试完HKUST,为了回报CD对我的巨大帮助，特发面经供后来人参考。

Part1: background&motivation
面试官：May, 非常nice的lady, 是HKUST MBA program consultant,整个过程好像是聊天，就着我的background问了些问题，也给我internship提了些建议。我觉得对我很有启发，很感激。36分钟

  1. from what you did to what you are doing, it is a big jump, right?
  2. does your past experience help you to do what you are doing?
  3. we have a professor XX, who is in line with your career goal, he might give you many helps.
    (这不是个问题，我们就着这个professor聊了一会儿）
  4. what other school you apply?
  5. any question for me?

Part2: potential
面试官：校友Frank, 大概30岁左右的香港人面孔，加拿大国籍。整个过程不卑不亢。23分钟

  1. why don't you start the conversation?
  2. correct me if i am wrong, your former experience has nothing to do with what you are doing. why?
  3. leadership style? examples?
  4. give you 30 seconds to tell me something i can remember you 3 days later
  5. any question for me?

值得注意的是，两个面试官都看过我的申请材料和essay.

就记起这些了，希望对后人有帮助。
