新鲜UW Foster MBA 香港面经 [2016-03-11]

攒人品赶紧发面经。今天刚在香港面了Foster，面试的是career development office的一个AD，她正好来香港和一些企业开会，就主动提出和我面试让我不用飞去上海。面试问题和之前一个全面的问题总结里的差不多：

  • What's your current job? Which part you like most / least?
  • What's your short / long term career plan? Which part you feel Foster can help you to achieve your goal? What kind of thing you think will ruin your whole plan?
  • Tell me a story that you got negative feedback from your boss?
  • What will be the most challenging part you will have in Foster in your mind? How will you digest it?
  • What will you do in your spare time?
  • Any questions for me?

面试官人很nice,并且自己也是Foster MBA毕业的所以分享了很多Foster如何改变她的职业规划甚至性格的故事，因为我们都是跑者，她还share了很多在Seattle running的经验。面试了大概45分钟，聊得非常愉快。

Foster的结果会在3月底出来，非常非常希望可以去啊！！！
