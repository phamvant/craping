R2康奈尔 MBA AO面经

正式开始面试前不知不觉居然扯了20分钟闲天， 聊了工作旅行， 星球大战，摄影器材（主要是尼康的大三元）

正式面试内容：
Do you enjoy your current job? Why?
Why mba now?
Short-term goal?
Backup plan for the short-term goal?
What Cornell can do to help you?
Did you attend any Cornell event? How did you learn aboutthe program?
What’s your leadership style like?
What can you bring to the Johnson Community?
How would your coworker describe you professionally?
Any questions for me?
面试完毕，用时大概一个小时左右。

希望能对大家准备有所帮助。
祝好！
