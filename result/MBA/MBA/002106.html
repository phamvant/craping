今年在 CD 上得到了很多小伙伴的友情帮助，非常感谢大家！我也分享一下申请麦马的过程。

Timeline：

  • 01.22 申请
  • 01.23 kira 邀请
  • 01.29 完成 kira
  • 02.06 二面邀请
  • 02.27 完成二面
  • 03.06 收到 offer

其中，kira 面试的题目如下：

  1. Why MBA, why DeGroot?
  2. Share a challenging situation working with a diverse group of individuals (i.e. age, gender, culture, and ethnicity). What was the challenge, and what did you do to address it?
  3. Tell us about your proudest accomplishment. More specifically, describe a project or activity that you led, and how did your leadership result in a positive impact on the business, organization or broader community.
  4. Share with us your most proud achievement as it relates to volunteer work or community engagement. What was your role, what did you do and what were the outcomes?
  5. 作文：不好意思记不太清了。好像和 motivation 有关。

以及，二面的题目如下：

  1. Why MBA/why you good fit?
  2. Resume questions.
  3. Leadership example.
  4. Teamwork example.
  5. Work beyond responsibility example.
  6. Failure example.
  7. Information analysis example.
  8. Behavioral Q: What would you do when you are co-working a project with a peer and the manager recognized many errors made by your peer?
  9. Anything else we should know.
  10. Any questions.

面试官基本全程都在低头记录你的回答（感觉是在考面试官啊，这速记能力），所以互动比较少。

BG:国内本科 985/211 非商科专业 4 年外资银行工作经历 GMAT 650 IELTS 7（6）

最后，希望大家的申请结果都能顺意，早日明朗。
