Tuck 面经 [2020.10.07]

时长：45 min 面试官：2nd year student,非常亲切的小哥。

  1. Tell me about yourself.
  2. Why Tuck?
  3. How would your teammates describe you?
  4. Share one harsh feedback you received and how did you react to it?
  5. What's your biggest achievement?
  6. What activities will you engage at Tuck?
  7. Any questions for me?
