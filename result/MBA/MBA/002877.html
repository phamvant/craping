Georgetown MBA R2台北面經 [2012-02-03]

2/3
今天下午的面試, 地點在台北的君悅飯店.面試者是女士Lynn Garnett, 貌似五十多歲, 為人非常nice.
面試氣氛非常輕鬆, 有點像在聊天.
面試題目如下：

  1. Short and long term goal.
  2. Tell me about your family/ activitiesyou do in your free time.
  3. Family business questions? (本人從事family business)
  4. Do you have conflict with employee?
  5. What`s your weakness?
  6. How you know the MBA programs?
  7. Tell me about your international experience.
  8. Any question for me?

結束了台北的面試後Lynn女士將飛往內地面試, 根據我的情報,前五題出的機率挺高, 另外international experience也要強調. 希望大家都可以上理想的學校.
