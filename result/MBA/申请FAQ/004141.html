问：在填写online application表格时，每个工作经验下面都让你描述 duties/responsibilities，这个应该是把job descriptions抄过来呢，还是copy paste 简历上的，就是说包括了一些achievements？

茉莉清茶 2010-10-05
答：简历基本上都是些工作成绩，不是纯粹的工作描述，所以我部分借鉴了简历，其他就按照实际的工作职责来写。

JonFrank 2010-10-06
答：Hey there!  No, no need to repeat your resume.  After all, why would they ask for the same thing twice! Here, they are looking for a bit more targeted information. So if on a resume, you list all ten things you do at a company, here you would only want to mention the main ones.  The big picture.  And of course you could talk about those main things in more detail, if the word limits allow.  But make it easy for them, so that they will understand who you are, and what you MAIN job is.  You will want to be more selective here, not less.

Hope this helps!
