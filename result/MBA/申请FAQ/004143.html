问关于NYU Stern essay的纸张格式要求

nkfingineer：

The following essay questions give you the opportunity to present yourself more fully to the Admissions Committee and to provide insight into your experiences, goals, and thought processes. Your essays should be written entirely by you. An offer of admission will be revoked if you did not write your essays. Please note the following:

  • Essays 1, 2, and 4 must be formatted for standard U.S. 8 1/2" x 11" paper and double-spaced, in 12-point font.
  • Please adhere to the essay word limits provided for each question.
  • Word limits apply to the total question. For example, your response to Essay 1 should answer part (a), part (b), and part (c) with a total maximum of 750 words.
  • Label the top of each essay with the following: Name, Date of Birth (Month, Day, Year), Essay Number, and Page Number [ex. Joe Applicant, January 1, 1983, Essay 1, Page 1]

这里面的纸张格式要求怎么达到？中文环境下word可以调成这种格式吗？

Zeros：

letter是北美比较通用的纸张大小，8.5"x11"，比A4略宽，略短一些。letter对应厘米是：21.59cm X 27.94cm，在中文office 2007中是“信纸（21.59厘米x27.94厘米）”（“纸张大小”的第一个选项）

nkfingineer

弱问，那在word 2003中，是不是只需将“纸张”调成 21.59*27.94就可以了？

那这个dould-space 12-pont是不是就完全是在“段落”里调成普通的两倍行距，字号调成12就可以了？然后字体任选？

Zeros：

手头上没有office 2003，我存了一个letter纸张大小的空文件，如果不介意，你可以直接用这个。或者，你可以在word 2003里找对应的纸张，符合：21.59厘米x27.94厘米，这个就是letter纸的尺寸。
