1 Education and Work Experience - Part 1
Your resume should highlight your work and educational history as you would present them to a prospective employer. Work experience should be listed starting with the most recent, including any military service you have had. Identify the names of employers, locations, positions held, responsibilities, dates of employment, and indicate full-time or part-time status. . 

2 Professional/Business Organizations - Part 2
Please list all professional and/or business organizations to which you belong and include your responsibilities, dates of involvement, positions held, and indicate whether the positions were elected or appointed.
