Post-Collegiate Activities
List your post-collegiate activities in order of their importance to you. Be sure to include the following information:

Dates
Name of Organization
Contact Phone Number
Activities
Number of Members
Hrs/Month
Office Held
Elected or Appointed to Office
哥大的申请资料里面没有要求resume，但有这个Post-Collegiate Activities，是不是就是resume呢？另外，文本格式,可编辑区域很小（比cd论坛的还窄一点），把resume放进去似乎非常杂乱，很难看。怎么办？
